# Farmable Items Guide

This guide explains how to obtain three essential farmable items in the Minepreggo mod: Lemons, Chili Peppers, and Cucumbers.

---

## Overview

These three items are food/crop resources that can be obtained through various methods and are useful for crafting craving foods and trading with villagers. All three items are **compostable**, meaning they can be used in composters to produce bone meal.

---

## Lemon

### How to Obtain

**From Leaves:**
- Break **Oak Leaves** or **Dark Oak Leaves** trees
- Each leaf block broken has a **7.5% chance** to drop 2-5 lemons
- More efficient with **Fortune Enchantment** on your tool
  - Fortune I: ~9.4% chance
  - Fortune II: ~11.8% chance
  - Fortune III: ~14.7% chance

**From Farmers:**
- Trade with **Farmer Villagers** (Level 1+)
- Buy: 2 emeralds per lemon
- Sell: 15 lemons for 1 emerald (bulk trade)

### Usage

- Ingredient in craving foods (Lemon Ice Cream, Lemon Drops, Lemon Ice Popsicles)
- Trading with villagers for emeralds
- Composting for bone meal

### Growing Tips

- Plant a small **oak tree farm** near your base
- Use Fortune III pickaxe or axe for maximum drops
- More efficient than farming from ground level
- Consider growing multiple trees for consistent supply

## Chili Pepper

### How to Obtain

**From Farmers:**
- Trade with **Farmer Villagers** at various levels
- Buy: 2 emeralds per pepper (Level 1), 4 emeralds per pepper (Level 2)
- Sell: 13 peppers for 1 emerald (Level 1), bulk at higher levels

**From Crops:**
- Chili peppers can be grown as crops
- Plant seeds to establish a pepper farm
- Harvest grown pepper plants

### Usage

- Ingredient in craving foods (Chili Poppers)
- Ingredient in **Hot Sauce** (crafting ingredient)
- Trading with villagers
- Composting for bone meal

### Growing Tips

- Establish a **dedicated pepper farm** for consistent supply
- Plant multiple crops for bulk harvesting
- Farmers will buy back excess peppers for emeralds
- More valuable when sold to farmers than raw crops

## Cucumber

### How to Obtain

**From Farmers:**
- Trade with **Farmer Villagers** at various levels
- Buy: 4 emeralds per cucumber (Level 1), 8 emeralds per cucumber (Level 2)
- Sell: 10 cucumbers for 1 emerald (Level 1), bulk at higher levels

**From Crops:**
- Cucumbers can be grown as crops
- Plant seeds to establish a cucumber farm
- Harvest grown cucumber plants

### Usage

- Ingredient in craving foods (Pickle)
- Ingredient in **Pickle** (crafting ingredient)
- Trading with villagers
- Composting for bone meal

### Growing Tips

- Establish a **dedicated cucumber farm** for consistent supply
- Plant in hydrated soil for optimal growth
- More efficient to buy from farmers at early game
- Save for pickling once you have enough supply

## Composting

All three items are **compostable** and can be used in composters:

### How to Compost

1. Place a **Composter** block in your world
2. Right-click with the item to add it to the composter
3. Items have a **30% chance** to increase the composter level
4. When full, right-click to get **Bone Meal**

### Why Compost

- Convert excess crops into bone meal
- Bone meal is used for:
  - Plant growth acceleration
  - Crafting recipes
  - Fertilizing crops
- Good way to dispose of surplus items

### Composting Efficiency

| Item | Compost Chance | Yield |
|------|---|---|
| Lemon | 30% | Contributes to bone meal |
| Chili Pepper | 30% | Contributes to bone meal |
| Cucumber | 30% | Contributes to bone meal |
