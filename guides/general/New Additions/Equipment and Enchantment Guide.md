# <a id="_dzzib4je1tt3"></a>__Equipment and Enchantment Guide__

This guide explains the special equipment and enchantments of Minepreggo mod, both their acquisition and effects\.

## <a id="_vikq7iysqf4g"></a>Overview

As entities become pregnant and have their belly swelling up, they can’t fit their big belly into normal armors, as it is getting in the way; This is where the mod’s special armors come into the spotlight, as they are the only way to fit the swelling bellies\.

There are also special enchantments that, while it does not work normally, activates during pregnancy, and assists in making the pregnancy safer\.

## <a id="_8hcutfx7thlc"></a>Equipments

### <a id="_48tehc6nlt5c"></a>Female Chestplates

These chestplates are designed specifically for females to cover their breasts, however it can be used by male entities to imagine they have a pair of them\. These are made by converting a normal chestplate, without any additional materials\. There is no difference in the protection it gives besides appearance\.

Like most chestplates, these get dropped off when the entities’ belly starts swelling\.

### <a id="_v7466fvosbap"></a>Maternity Chestplates

The special chestplates designed to cover big bellies are essential for female entities to protect themselves, though male entities can also use them to imagine they are pregnant with a big belly\. These are made by adding materials onto the chestplate with the same base material, with the amount deciding the size of the belly it can cover\. There is no difference in the protection it gives\.

When the belly exceeds the size it can cover, when the Lactation effect is active, or when giving birth, it will be dropped off\. At pregnancy phase 5 onwards, the belly will be huge enough that maternity chestplates can no longer cover it\.

### <a id="_eh9rw48eqkzt"></a>Belly Shields

A shield tied with rope to cover the front of the belly is the only available protection for female entities at late pregnancy\. These are made by adding rope onto a shield, with the amount of ropes used deciding the size of the belly it can cover\. Regardless of size, it gives only 4 armor, but also a 5% knockback resistance\.

When the belly exceeds the size it can cover or when giving birth, it will be dropped off\. As pregnancy phase 8 is the maximum, the player can prepare the biggest belly shield and wear it from the beginning\.

### <a id="_8xgjhjz0dix6"></a>Knee Braces

These special knee pads are designed to protect pregnant entities while their butt swelled up and drops off most leggings\. These are made with two ropes and one material\.

In terms of protection, the leather and golden variant give 1 armor, the chainmail variant \(uncraftable\) gives 2 armor, and the iron variant gives 3 armor\. The diamond variant gives 5 armor and 0 armor toughness, unlike the diamond leggings\. However, the Netherite variant, while giving 0 armor toughness and knockback resistance, gives 7 armor instead of 6 from the netherite leggings\.

As with maternity chestplates and belly shields, these are dropped off when giving birth\.

## <a id="_onyrqhhsxx9o"></a>Enchantments

Unlike most enchantments, these maternity enchantments cannot be found via enchanting table, fishing or looting from chests\. The only way to acquire these is from Scientific Illagers, which always spawn with one trade that sells a maternity enchanted book\. The player can purchase those with emeralds and a book, and apply those with an anvil\.

### <a id="_oznrzccnvwok"></a>Pregnancy Protection

As one of the maternity defensive enchantments, this is only supported by maternity chestplates or belly shields\. Unlike most protective enchantments, this can co\-exist with other protection enchantments such as Fire Protection or Blast Protection\.

This initially brings no effect when applied\. When the wearer is pregnant, however, the enchantment reduces the pregnancy damage taken, making miscarriage less likely to happen\.

This enchantment has up to 4 levels, which affect the degree of reduction\. However, for unknown reasons, any level of this enchantment completely nullifies pregnancy damage when pregnant\.

### <a id="_rj9u10vjfsu3"></a>Pregnancy Knockback Resistance

As one of the maternity defensive enchantments, this is only supported by maternity chestplates and belly shields\. Like Pregnancy Protection, this can co\-exist with other protection enchantments\.

This initially brings no effect when applied\. When the wearer is, however, the enchantment provides knockback resistance, which increases along the pregnancy, making the wearer more difficult to be moved by attacks\.

This enchantment only has one single level\. Increasing its level in any way does not affect the effect\.

### <a id="_sqbwn8g0k43f"></a>Fetal Bond

One of the maternity offensive enchantments, this is only supported by melee weapons such as swords and axes\. Like most damage enchantments, this cannot co\-exist with other damage enchantments like Sharpness or Smite\.

This initially brings no effect when applied\. When the user is pregnant and their belly starts swelling, however, the enchantment gives the ability to deal increased damage\. The damage increases for every phase since phase 1\.

This enchantment only has one single level\. Increasing its level in any way does not affect the effect\.
