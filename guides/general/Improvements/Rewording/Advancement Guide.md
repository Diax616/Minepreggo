# **Advancements Guide**

This guide provides an overview of all the advancements available in the Minepreggo mod v1.3.1.

## Starter Advancements

The advancements do not show up initially; Instead, the player needs to first acquire the first advancement of the advancement tree. Each gender has a separate tree.

### Starting: Future Father or Future Mother?

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player must pick their gender at the start of the world.

This is the beginning of the mod's advancements.

### Female Branch: I'm Pregnant?

To obtain this advancement, the player must be a female, and get pregnant through either player sex, villager sex, attacks from zombies, creepers, or endermen, or through potions or the Reproduction Witch. The type and number of babies do not matter.

This is the starting point for most pregnancy-related advancements.

### Male Branch: Take Responsibility

To obtain this advancement, the player must be a male, and impregnate any female entities, including but not limited to female players, zombie girls, creeper girls and slime girls (humanoid or monster), and ender girls. The type and number of babies do not matter.

This marks the beginning of the father-related advancement tree.

### Shared Branch: Interested in the End

To obtain this advancement, the player must obtain a Concentrated Ender Cum Specimen Tube. The gender of the player does not matter.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the Adventure section of vanilla Minecraft advancements.

## Female Branch Advancements

Once the player gets pregnant, they will gain access to the entire advancement tree. The sequence does not matter, so one can acquire them in any order.

### First Time Mother

To obtain this advancement, the player must successfully be impregnated through any means and successfully give birth without death or miscarriage during pregnancy, with either losing the babies in the process. The type and number of babies do not matter.

### Serial Mother

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player must enter a pregnancy 15 times. The outcome does not matter.

### No Place Like Womb

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player must be giving birth successfully on all three vanilla dimensions: Overworld, the Nether, and the End.

### Magic Pregnancy

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to be impregnated with potion effects, whether it is from a potion or an arrow. The type or number of babies does not matter.

### Know Your Body

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, simply conduct a fertility checkup from a Doctor or a Scientific Illager.

### Two Lines

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to conduct a pregnancy test checkup, and have the result being positive.

### Good Mother

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to complete all types of prenatal checkups on themselves. This can be done from a Doctor or a Scientific Illager.

As players cannot do checkups without being pregnant, they must be impregnated before attempting.

### Bad or Good Mother?

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to conduct excess baby reduction from a Scientific Illager. As this is only possible if the womb is overloaded, potions of Baby Duplication are required.

### Bad Mother I

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to trigger Fetal Movement V effect via attacking their own belly. As only pregnancy phase 3 onwards can trigger Fetal Movement, the player needs to be pregnant, and enable the belly collisions while doing so.

### Bad Mother II

_Originally named Bad Mother in v1.2.10._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to sell a baby item born by self to a Scientific Illager. The type of babies does not matter.

### Breeder of the Village

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to be impregnated by a villager naturally. The type or number of babies afterwards do not matter.

Cheating by using Villager Impregnation potions won't trigger the advancement.

### New Member for the Village

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to use a baby villager on the villager that impregnated the player.

As pregnancy through potions does not register a villager, babies from such are not usable for this advancement.

### Brewing an Ender Dragon Potion

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to brew a potion of Ender Dragon Impregnation.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the End section of vanilla Minecraft advancements.

### Pregnant with a Dragon

To complete this challenge, the player needs to be impregnated with an Ender Dragon baby. The way of such does not matter.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the End section of vanilla Minecraft advancements.

### Give Birth a Dragon

To complete this challenge, the player needs to successfully endure and give birth to the Ender Dragon baby. Any potion usage in the period is allowed. Due to the rarity of the potion itself, the player was not permitted death in this period, or the challenge will be considered incompletable without uses of cheats.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the End section of vanilla Minecraft advancements.

### Fertile Mother

To complete this challenge, the player needs to be impregnated with either player or villager sex, or from attacks of zombies, creepers, endermen or slimes, and has 8 babies as a result. The type of babies does not matter.

Cheating by using Impregnation potions or Baby Duplication potions won't trigger the advancement.

_Since v1.3.0, only 3 babies are needed to trigger the advancement._

### Give Birth by All Species

To complete this challenge, the player needs to give birth to human, villager, zombie, creeper, enderman, slime, and the Ender Dragon at least once each. The way to be impregnated does not matter.

Due to the difficulty of interspecies pregnancy and the rarity of ender dragon pregnancy, it is recommended to challenge this as the final objective of the mod.

## Male Branch Advancements

Once the player impregnates an entity, they will gain access to the entire advancement tree. The sequence does not matter, so one can acquire them in any order.

### It's Over

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to use a specimen tube on themselves and obtain Cum Specimen Tube while being Infertile.

### Stud

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to impregnate a female entity 20 times. The outcome does not matter.

### Fertile Father

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to impregnate a female entity and has 3 or more babies as a result.

### Present at Birth

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to be around the female entity that they impregnated during the birth process.

### Sprint to Birth

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, upon the female is experiencing water breaking, the player needs to reach the female entity from over 100 blocks away before the birth process starts. Any method for such is allowed.

### Good Father

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to conduct all types of prenatal checkup for their tamed pregnant PreggoMob.

### Bad Father I

_This is added in v1.3.0._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to trigger early birth on the female entity via a sex event.

### Bad Father II

_Originally named Bad Father in v1.2.10._

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to sell a baby item born by the entity that's impregnated by themselves to a Scientific Illager. The type of babies does not matter.

### Impregnate All Species

To complete this challenge, the player needs to impregnate female players, zombie girls, creeper girls and slime girls (both humanoid and monster), and ender girls. The outcome of pregnancy does not matter.

Unlike most of the advancements of this mod, this is **incompletable** under single player.

## PreggoMobs Related Advancements

Advancements below are not limited to gender and can be completed by either. Most of them are added since v1.3.0.

### Zombie Tamer

To obtain this advancement, simply tame a Zombie Girl.

### Creeper Tamer

To obtain this advancement, simply tame a Monster Creeper Girl.

### Humanoid Creeper Tamer

To obtain this advancement, simply tame a Humanoid Creeper Girl.

### Ender Tamer

To obtain this advancement, simply tame an Ender Girl.

### Slime Girl Tamer (Monster)

To obtain this advancement, simply tame a Monster Slime Girl.

### Slime Girl Tamer (Humanoid)

To obtain this advancement, simply tame a Humanoid Slime Girl.

### How Could I Say No?

To obtain this advancement, the player must accept a sex request from a PreggoMob. As PreggoMobs can only initiate a sex request when having Horny effect, they must be pregnant before doing so.

### Sunscreen

To obtain this advancement, the player must equip a Zombie Girl with a helmet to counter their zombie traits of fear of sunlight.

### Waterproofing

To obtain this advancement, the player must equip an Ender Girl with a helmet to counter their enderman traits of fear of rain.

### Dimensional Traveler

To obtain this advancement, the player must go through all vanilla dimensions; Overworld, the Nether and the End, with a PreggoMob that's in the last phase of the pregnancy.

## Gender Unrestricted Advancements

Advancements below are not limited to gender and can be completed by either. Most of them are added since v1.3.0.

### Dead End

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to receive Infertile V effect. Any methods are allowed.

### Black Market

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to purchase a PreggoMob from a Scientific Illager.

### Valued Customer

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to finish the supply of one trade of Scientific Illager. The item in question does not matter.

### Questionable Sponsor

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to fully finish the supply of all trades of a Scientific Illager.

### Scientific Killer

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to kill the Scientific Illager, without killing any Ill PreggoMob with him.

### Breast Milk Connoisseur

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to drink all types of breast milk. This includes Human, Zombie, Creeper, Ender and Slime.

As the corresponding breast milk are only obtainable from female player and PreggoMobs, the player needs to tame every type of such. Unlike most of the advancements of this mod, this is **incompletable** under single player without a gender change (to obtain Human Breast Milk Bottle).

### Not in My Womb

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to defeat 10 Fertility Witches. Only the Witches defeated by a player's attack counts.

### Keep It Alive

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to defeat 10 Infertility Witches. Only the Witches defeated by a player's attack counts.
