# **Medical Checkups Guide**

This guide explains how medical checkups work in the Minepreggo mod v1.3.1, including how to activate them, who provides them, and what benefits they offer.

## Overview

Medical checkups are special services offered by Doctors or Scientific Illagers to help monitor and assess pregnancy. During pregnancy, players and tamed pregnant mobs can visit these NPCs to receive valuable information and items related to their pregnancy status.

## Types of Medical Checkups

There are two different types of medical checkups available, with up to 6 services in total. All of them cost emeralds and can be done by either a Doctor or a Scientific Illager. However, the Doctor **cannot do** any of them on preggo mobs.

### Fertility Test

_This is added since v1.3.0._

This type of checkup is only accessible when the patient is not pregnant or is a male. There are two services:

#### Fertility State

This informs the user about their fertility score, reproduction health, their current menstrual phase (for females), and whether they are fertile. For females, it also informs on the current menstrual cycle phase, eggs for this cycle, whether they are ovulating, and the time taken for ovulation starts or fertile window. For males, it informs sperm quality, calculated from the viability and concentration. If both conduct the checkup together, extra information about the fertilization rate will be added.

#### Pregnancy Test

This detects whether the user is ongoing in the fertilization process. This is only available to a female entity.

### Prenatal Checkup

This type of checkup is only accessible when the patient is pregnant. There are four services:

#### Pregnancy State

This informs the user about the current and last pregnancy phase, pregnancy health, and days left before the next phase or giving birth. At least pregnancy phase 1 is required.

#### Ultrasound Scan

This detects the baby count, belly safety and the baby species and gender. At least pregnancy phase 2 is required.

#### Paternity Test

This detects the entity that impregnated the user. If there is no entity (which happens with potion/mob attack pregnancy), none will be stated. At least pregnancy phase 3 is required.

#### Excess Baby Reduction

_This is added since v1.3.0._

This removes the excess babies if the entity is overloaded. This can only be done by a Scientific Illager, and at least pregnancy phase 3 is required.
