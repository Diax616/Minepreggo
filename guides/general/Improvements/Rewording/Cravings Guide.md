# Pregnancy Cravings Guide

This guide explains the pregnancy craving system and all the special foods that satisfy different types of cravings in the Minepreggo mod v1.3.1.

## Overview

During pregnancy, players and PreggoMobs experience cravings for specific types of foods. These cravings are categorized by flavor profile and are specific to the species being carried. Eating the right type of food to satisfy active cravings provides gratification that reduces the craving meter.

The flavor that the player wants will be shown when the craving meter reaches 20. Attack is reduced by 20% when the craving meter reaches 20, lasting until the craving meter reaches back to 2 or below. For PreggoMobs, the flavor will be shown when the craving meter reaches 16 (where Craving effect is applied), and a warning message will be triggered on reaching 20.

### Notes

There are 4 types of flavors that satisfy different cravings, and all foods are assigned to be only edible to one of the four species. However, players can eat the food of other species and have them as craving if they are pregnant with said species and at pregnancy phase 3 onwards, with penalty applied.
