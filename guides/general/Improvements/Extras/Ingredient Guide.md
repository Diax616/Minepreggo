# **Ingredient Guide**

This guide explains how to obtain essential ingredients in the Minepreggo mod v1.3.1.

## Overview

These items are food/crop resources that can be obtained through various methods and are useful for crafting craving foods and trading with villagers.

## Lemon

These sour fruits are naturally found as drops from Oak Leaves, either those that got broken or naturally decayed. These can also be traded from, or to a Farmer.

On consumption, it recovers 1 hunger and 1 saturation. It can also be used for composting, with a 60% chance of adding one layer of compost.

It is the main ingredient to craft all sour foods.

## Chili Pepper

These spicy vegetables are usually traded from, or to a Farmer. Afterwards, they can be planted in a similar manner to carrots and potatoes.

On consumption, it recovers 1 hunger and 1 saturation. It can also be used for composting, with a 60% chance of adding one layer of compost.

It is the ingredient of Hot Sauce, the main ingredient to craft most spicy cravings for pregnancy.

## Cucumber

These green vegetables are usually traded from, or to a Farmer. Afterwards, they can be planted in a similar manner to carrots and potatoes.

On consumption, it recovers 2 hunger and 1 saturation. It can also be used for composting, with a 70% chance of adding one layer of compost.

It is the ingredient of pickles, one of the salty foods for players.

## Chocolate

These sweet snacks are crafted with 3 cocoa beans and 1 sugar, with 2 products per crafting attempt.

On consumption, it recovers 4 hunger and 1 saturation. If it's consumed by a pregnant player for sweet craving, it also recovers 20 gratification.

It is also the main ingredient to craft all other sweet foods for preggo mobs.

## Salt

These salty seasonings are found by smelting a water bucket, with 32 salts produced per smelting attempt.

It is the main ingredient to craft all salty foods.

## Brains

This favourite food of zombies are found as drops from villagers, illagers, and witches (either the vanilla witches or Reproduction Witches), traded from a Scientific Illager, or for villager brains, as loot from chests.

This cannot be eaten by players, unless they are pregnant with zombie babies, and it can be used to tame and feed zombie girls, which recover different hunger and saturation:

- Villager brain - 12 hunger + 5 saturation
- Illager brain - 8 hunger + 3 saturation
- Witch brain - 10 hunger + 4 saturation

These are the main ingredients to craft all foods for zombie girls.

## Activated Gunpowder

This reactive substance is crafted with gunpowder and redstone, but they can also be found as loot from chests.

This cannot be eaten by players, unless they are pregnant with creeper babies, and it can be used to tame and feed creeper girls, whichever variants, which recover 8 hunger and 1 saturation.

This is the main ingredient to craft all foods for creeper girls.

## Ender Slime Jelly

This blaze infused slimy food is crafted with slime block and 3 blaze powder, but they can also be found as loot from chests.

This cannot be eaten by players, unless they are pregnant with ender babies, and it can be used to tame and feed ender girls, which recover 10 hunger and 4 saturation.

This, along with the chorus fruit, is the main ingredient to craft all foods for ender girls.

## Refined Chorus Shards

This unique food is crafted with popped chorus fruit, blaze powder and golden nugget.

This cannot be eaten by players, unless they are pregnant with ender babies, or in later phases of being pregnant with Ender Dragon baby, and it can be used to tame and feed ender girls, which recover 7 hunger and 5 saturation, and give a short period of Absorption effect.

Its main purpose is to act as base ingredient for concentrated ender cum specimen tube, the required item for Ender Dragon pregnancy.

## Stuffed Mushroom

_This is added since v1.3.0._

This special mushroom is crafted with a mushroom and a spider eye.

This cannot be eaten by players, unless they are pregnant with slime babies, and it can be used to tame and feed slime girls, which recover 4 hunger and 1 saturation.

This cannot be used for cravings.
