# **Body, Physics and Visuals Guide**

This guide explains the body parts and their physics for female entities, along with the visual effects in the Minepreggo mod v1.3.1.

## Overview

The physics system of the mod adds dynamic bouncing and movement animations to female body parts during gameplay and reacts based on the actions. There is a total of 3 main body parts added for this mod: Breasts, Buttocks and Belly.

These jiggle physics are **not** configurable and **cannot** be disabled. The animations and bounce characteristics are built-in and cannot be customized through configuration files or settings, except directly switching the mod code.

## Breasts

Breasts are naturally on the model of female players and PreggoMobs, on the chest part of the model. These motherly spheres jiggle whether the entities conduct horizontal or vertical movement. For horizontal movement, the jiggle intensity depends on the speed of movement.

Breasts start to swell when the entity is pregnant, with the size increase triggered on every phase since phase 3. They also swell noticeably when the entities have the Lactation effect, which drops chestplates off, except belly shields.

_Since v1.3.1, after giving birth, breasts shrink to the size of phase 4, lasting until the Maternity effect ends, in which they return to normal size._

## Buttocks

Buttocks are on the back part of the model of female players and PreggoMobs, behind the legs part of the model. Unlike other body parts, these doors of birth only jiggle whether the entities conduct vertical movement, and show low, or no movement for horizontal movement.

Unlike breasts, buttocks do not appear for the entities but only start to appear and swell when the entity is pregnant, with the size increase triggered on every phase since phase 3, which drops most leggings off. After giving birth, they shrink back to their normal size (which is none).

## Belly

Belly is on the front part of the model of female players and PreggoMobs, below the chest part of the model. This container of babies jiggles whether the entities conduct horizontal or vertical movement. Just like breasts, the jiggle intensity depends on the speed of movement for horizontal movement.

However, the belly has more physics than other two body parts, as the babies reside there; Their movement may increase rapidly with visible kicks when Fetal Movement effect is active, and especially at huge size, the belly also has a brief size changing sequence, which indicates the babies' and the mother's breathing.

Unlike breasts, the belly does not appear for the entities but only start to appear and swell when the entity is pregnant. Unlike other body parts, however, the belly swells on every phase after phase 0, making its size enormous at the end of pregnancy, and drops most chest protections off. The belly shrinks during birth based on the number of babies born and return to normal size (which is none) after all babies are born.

_Since v1.3.1, the belly size now ties to both phase and baby count, and can go further than what was safe to not trigger a belly burst._

## Visuals

_This is added in v1.3.0._

### Morning Sickness

For morning sickness, a green tint identical to nausea's effect appears on the screen, along with a distortion. Both are affected by the intensity.

### Horny

For horny, a red tint is used instead, however with the mod's custom texture.
