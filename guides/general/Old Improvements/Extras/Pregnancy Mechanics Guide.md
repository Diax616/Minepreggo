# <a id="_j2g2lvpqblbu"></a>__Pregnancy Mechanics Guide__

This guide explains how the pregnancy system works in the Minepreggo mod in simple terms\.

## <a id="_1naral3hst8m"></a>Overview

For both female players and preggo mobs, the pregnancy starts with a fertilisation process, which is done with actions or potions, followed by a brief period \(if natural fertilisation\), and the pregnancy starts afterwards\. This is followed by a set of days, split into several phases depending on the number of babies, with pregnancy symptoms arising and amplifying, giving birth at the end\. After giving birth, the mothers have a brief period that cannot be pregnant, and the cycle loops after\.

Time used for these steps is in the configuration of the mod\. Check the Configuration Guide for more\.

## <a id="_1q4f5764vef7"></a>Impregnation

There are several ways a female entity can become pregnant in this mod:

### <a id="_dzlrt18bx7bo"></a>Sexual Intercourse

Female players can initiate sex towards male players\. Male players, meanwhile, can initiate sex towards any female entities\. Both are followed by a short cutscene\. Afterwards, players involved will be having a period of Weakness and Hunger II effect\. Preggo mobs involved will be having a period of Weakness effect only\.

Certain conditions must be met before having sex:

- No hostile mobs \(this includes untamed preggo mobs\) are in a 16\-block range
- The targets must be less than 32 blocks away
- A bed must be present in 8 block range
- Either target is not giving birth or having sex
- Either target must not have sex for a while before

### <a id="_kipml83frhoc"></a>Mob Attack

Female players can be impregnated by specific mobs, including zombies \(includes husks, drowneds or zombie villagers\), creepers and endermen\.

In 1\.2\.10, female players have a 50% chance to be impregnated by mob attacks if they are having the Fertile effect\.

In 1\.3\.0, the chance of impregnation depends on the attack count and menstrual cycle\.

### <a id="_24bdzloly1pg"></a>Villager Sex

For female players, they can initiate sex with villagers, but it works differently; The player must throw food at a villager with a profession to initiate sex\. Most other conditions are applied\.

After a short cutscene, the player will be having a period of Weakness and Hunger II effect, like sex with players, while the villager will be having a period of Slowness effect instead\.

For more information, check Other Species Pregnancy Guide\.

### <a id="_i873c5g2gc6b"></a>Potion Effects

Acquire Impregnation\-type potion effects in any way \(potions, arrows or commands\) and have them expire will trigger pregnancy\.

Unlike other methods, this does not need a brief period for fertilisation\.

## <a id="_rba8pkvl64b7"></a>Fertilisation

After any method above, if the process turned out to be successful, the female entities will experience a brief period for the calculation process, during which they will randomly have a brief period of Nausea effect\.

After this period, and if the entities do not die in between, they will be having Pregnancy \(P0\) effect, where the adventure really starts\!

## <a id="_d13yjopa2siq"></a>Pregnancy

### <a id="_a47bme9yt8y4"></a>Phase 0

The beginning of pregnancy is nothing special; No bulge of belly yet, no symptoms, nothing changed except the feeling of life growing inside\. The entities may feel hungry when idle, however, hence prepare more food for the pregnancy\.

This doesn’t mean pregnant entities are not safe of danger; Death at any point during the pregnancy will lead to a failed pregnancy\. While no side effects will happen, the babies carried will be converted to dead fetuses, depending on species of babies\. Exercise caution\.

### <a id="_ftdc5bscbx2g"></a>Phase 1

This is where the pregnancy effects start to reveal itself\. Pregnant entities’ belly starts to swell, making most chestplates __unusable__\. The only chestplates that are applicable are maternity chestplates, made with original chestplates\. Idle hunger also increased 18% faster than last phase, and pregnancy healing got a 5% decrease\. Pregnant entities also feel 1% more exhaustion, that is, hunger from actions, which stacks with idle hunger\.

Pregnant entities also experience __cravings__, one of the main effects of pregnancy\. Along the way, the craving meter will increase overtime, and once it reaches 20 \(player\) or 16 \(preggo mobs\), the flavor wanted will be shown, and the entities must eat food of said flavor to gain gratification and reduce the craving meter\. Attack is reduced by 20% on Craving effect triggers, lasting until the craving meter reaches 2 or below\. For more information, check Cravings Guide\.

Pregnant entities may also experience __morning sickness__, a side effect of pregnancy\. Every second, there’s a 0\.1% chance to receive 30 seconds of Morning Sickness effect, which is accompanied by Nausea effect of the same duration\. Attack speed is reduced by 25% during this period\.

The bulging belly also brings danger against mobs, where pregnant entities lose __pregnancy health __when being attacked by other mobs when health is below 60%\. Regular sleep and potions are crucial to prevent careless mistakes\. During this phase, pregnant entities take 1 pregnancy damage when this is triggered\.

### <a id="_pj9j04uyz0xx"></a>Phase 2

As the belly starts to swell further, the weight of the belly starts causing __gravity__ to take over, where the pregnant entities feel 10% more gravity than before, as well as 15% slower movement\. Basic maternity chestplates made for phase 1 bellies are also no longer usable\. Idle hunger increased 25% faster than during conception, exhaustion increased by 2%, and pregnancy healing got a 10% decrease\.

For symptoms, cravings now trigger 11% faster, and morning sickness triggers at 0\.15% chance\.

Breasts also are responding to baby growth, which pregnant entities now experience __lactation__, one of the main effects of pregnancy\. Along the way, the milking meter will increase overtime \(which swells the breasts\), and entities need to milk themselves with glass bottles, which reduce the meter by 5\. When it reaches 13, Lactation effect triggers, where movement speed is reduced by 10%, lasting until it reaches 5 or below\. Chestplates are not usable at all \(except belly shields\) during the Lactation effect due to the swollen breasts\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-2 pregnancy damage when receiving attacks\.

### <a id="_aj2p223vro3p"></a>Phase 3

Pregnant entities’ belly now swells to a noticeable size that makes maternity chestplates made for phase 2 not usable\. Pregnant entities also feel 12\.5% more gravity than before pregnancy due to the belly’s weight, 27% slower movement and 10% slower attacks\. Idle hunger increased 33% faster than during conception, exhaustion increased by 3% and pregnancy healing got a 15% decrease\.

For symptoms, cravings now trigger 18% faster than when they first appear, milk now produces 33% faster, and morning sickness triggers at 0\.2% chance\.

Besides belly and breasts, buttocks are now also affected by pregnancy; Its swelling causes leggings to be __unusable__, leaving knee braces the only leg protection available\. The entire weight of the body makes it that jumping and sprinting are also actions that need to be __avoided __to avoid discomfort\. When pregnant entities jump 45 times in total, or sprint for 200 seconds in total, they will have a brief period of Nausea effect\.

However, even if the pregnant entities do not conduct any activity, the weight of the body __still__ leaves a toll on them; Every 237 seconds, there is a 40% chance of receiving a period of Weakness effect\. Not only that, but the babies’ properties also spread to their mothers, which is dangerous for mothers carrying babies that are not human or villagers; They possess side effects that make the pregnancy __more dangerous__ and increase the frequency of Weakness effect triggers as well as the exhaustion from actions\. For more information, check Other Species Pregnancy Guide\.

This is also the period where baby movement started to be noticeable, named __fetal movement__\. For every tick, there is a 0\.15% chance to trigger Fetal Movement effect, where the babies are rapidly moving in the belly for 60 seconds\. The chances are different based on conditions; It increases to 0\.75% if the Eternal Pregnancy effect is active, or when the pregnant entity is riding another entity\. Carrying non\-human babies also increases fetal movement rate\. Movement speed is reduced by 20% and attack is reduced by 10% while babies are moving\.

Babies in the belly also lead to pregnant entities experiencing the need of __belly rub__, one of the main effects of pregnancy\. Along the way, the belly rub meter will increase overtime, and the entities need to rub their belly, which reduces the meter by 4\. When it reaches 12, Belly Rubs effect triggers, where luck is reduced by 100, lasting until it reaches 8 or below\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-3 pregnancy damage when receiving attacks\.

### <a id="_jt9fwcusr792"></a>Phase 4

The belly has swollen big enough that most, except the biggest maternity chestplates, are unusable\. Pregnant entities also feel 15% more gravity, 30% slower movement, 20% slower attacks, 43% more idle hunger, 4% more exhaustion and 20% less pregnancy healing than during conception\. Not only that, but the weight of the belly has also made it that pregnant entities take 5% less knockback from attacks, making escaping harder\.

For symptoms, cravings now trigger 25% faster than the first time, milk produces 43% faster, belly rubs requirements are 43% more frequently, fetal movement triggers at every 0\.9% per tick when riding, now lasts for 70 seconds, and random weakness possibly triggers every 236 seconds\. Morning sickness, however, remains the same frequency\. Pregnant entities now feel nauseous after 40 jumps or 190 seconds of sprinting instead\. Not only that, but the weight of the belly also now causes discomfort when sneaking; The same nausea applies after 150 seconds of sneaking\.

As the babies are close to being born, the womb starts to conduct __contraction__\. Every tick, there’s a 0\.2% for Contraction effect to trigger, where the pregnant entities may experience random pain for 40 seconds\. The chance increases to 1\.6% when riding\. Movement speed is reduced by 30% and attack is reduced by 20% while the womb is contracting\.

The idea of getting close to the due date also brings pregnant entities desires, making them feel __horny__\. This is one of the main effects of pregnancy, where the horny meter will increase overtime, and once it reaches 17, Horny effect will trigger, which doubles the detection range of mobs\. This is removed after sex, where horny meter also reduces to 0\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-5 pregnancy damage when receiving attacks\. After this phase, the pregnant entities will give birth if they are carrying one baby, regardless of species\. For those carrying more than one baby, they will proceed to the next phase\.

### <a id="_dqim62dgi748"></a>Phase 5

The belly has swollen to a huge size that all maternity chestplates are __incompatible__; The only way to gain protection for the chest now is belly shields, made with just shields and ropes\. These are not comparable to maternity chestplates in terms of protection, hence rendering pregnant entities more vulnerable\. Pregnant entities also experience 17\.5% more gravity, 41% slower movement, 30% slower attacks, 54% more idle hunger, 5% more exhaustion and 25% less pregnancy healing, and 10% less knockback\.

Cravings now trigger 33% faster than first time, with milk production and belly rubs requirements being 54% higher\. Fetal movement now triggers at 0\.2% per tick, increases to 1% with Eternal Pregnancy effect and 1\.4% when riding, lasting for 80 seconds, while contraction triggers at 1\.8% per tick when riding, lasting for 50 seconds\. Random weakness triggers every 235 seconds, while morning sickness remains at the same frequency, however pregnant entities now feel nauseous at 35 jumps, 180 seconds of sprinting, or 140 seconds of sneaking\. Pregnant entities also feel their sexual desires 54% more frequently\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-7 pregnancy damage on receiving attacks\. After this phase, the pregnant entities will give birth if they are carrying two or three babies, regardless of species\. For those carrying more than three babies, they will proceed to the next phase\.

### <a id="_ux8joh98chor"></a>Phase 6

The belly is now huge enough that small belly shields are no longer usable\. Pregnant entities also experience 20% more gravity, 45% slower movement, 40% slower attacks, 67% more idle hunger, 6% more exhaustion, 30% less pregnancy healing, and 15% less knockback\. Under default settings, the belly is now huge enough to dismount pregnant entities, and too heavy for taking flight with elytra\.

Cravings now trigger 43% faster than first time, with milk production and belly rubs requirements being 67% higher\. Fetal movement now triggers at 1\.6% per tick when riding, lasting for 90 seconds, while contraction triggers at 2% per tick when riding, with both now lasting for 60 seconds\. Random weakness triggers every 234 seconds, while morning sickness remains at the same frequency, however pregnant entities now feel nauseous at 30 jumps, 170 seconds of sprinting, or 130 seconds of sneaking\. Pregnant entities also feel their sexual desires 67% more frequently\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-9 pregnancy damage when receiving attacks\. After this phase, the pregnant entities will give birth if they are carrying four or five babies, regardless of species\. For those carrying more than five babies, they will proceed to the next phase\.

### <a id="_5f6kywcxqgqq"></a>Phase 7

The belly is now so heavy that only big belly shields are usable as protection\. Pregnant entities also experience 25% more gravity, 60% slower movement, 50% slower attacks, 81% more idle hunger, 7% more exhaustion, 35% less pregnancy healing, and 20% less knockback\.

Cravings now trigger 54% faster than first time, with milk production and belly rubs requirements being 81% higher\. Fetal movement now triggers at 1\.8% per tick when riding, lasting for 100 seconds, while contraction triggers at 2\.2% per tick when riding, with both now lasting for 70 seconds\. Random weakness triggers every 233 seconds, while morning sickness remains at the same frequency, however pregnant entities now feel nauseous at 25 jumps, 160 seconds of sprinting, or 120 seconds of sneaking\. Pregnant entities also feel their sexual desires 81% more frequently\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-11 pregnancy damage when receiving attacks\. After this phase, the pregnant entities will give birth if they are carrying six or seven babies, regardless of species\. For those carrying more than seven babies, they will proceed to the next, and final safe phase\.

### <a id="_ofm4szshuaoy"></a>Phase 8

The belly is swollen to the point that it’s out of bounds, rendering all chest protection, except the biggest belly shields, unusable as protection\. Pregnant entities also experience 30% more gravity, 75% slower movement, 60% slower attacks, 100% more idle hunger, 8% more exhaustion, 40% less pregnancy healing, and 25% less knockback\.

Cravings now trigger 67% faster than first time, with milk production and belly rubs requirements being 100% higher\. Fetal movement now triggers at 2% per tick when riding, lasting for 110 seconds, while contraction triggers at 2\.4% per tick when riding, with both now lasting for 80 seconds\. Random weakness triggers every 232 seconds, while morning sickness remains at the same frequency, however pregnant entities now feel nauseous at 20 jumps, 150 seconds of sprinting, or 110 seconds of sneaking\. Pregnant entities also feel their sexual desires 100% more frequently\.

Pregnant entities also take 1\-14 pregnancy damage when receiving attacks\. Due to the massive restrictions on moving and high frequency of symptoms, it is recommended to stay in the base in preparation of giving birth\. After this phase, the pregnant entities will give birth if they are carrying eight babies, regardless of species\.

## <a id="_pv1cu897s88"></a>Outcome

### <a id="_l9vfy9a1vp4f"></a>Birth

If the pregnant entity survived the entire pregnancy with pregnancy health always being above 1, and carrying 8 or less babies, they will experience water breaking for a minute, where movement speed is reduced by 30% and attack is reduced by 20%\. This is followed by a pre\-birth phase where the womb contracts for preparation\. This lasts for 30 seconds if the pregnancy ends at phase 4, or 40/45/50/55 seconds for phase 5/6/7/8, respectively\. After that, the birth process begins, where the chestplate and leggings that are currently wearing, as well as the items that are currently being held, will be dropped off\.

For preggo mobs, the birth duration differs based on the pregnancy phase, which is 60/65/70/75/80 seconds at phase 4/5/6/7/8\. For players, they will be playing a minigame where they must spam click the push button to push the baby out and repeat until all babies are born\. This is followed by 10 seconds of post birth per baby for either mother\.

Afterwards\. The mothers gain the Rehabilitation effect, where they cannot be pregnant and need time to rest\. Movement speed is reduced by 10% and health is reduced by 20% until the effect wears off\. All symptoms during pregnancy are also removed, except for milking, which is identified with the Maternity effect \(Lactation effect still triggers when condition is met\)\. The new mothers also gain 3 minutes of Weakness effect and 10 minutes of Luck effect\.

### <a id="_2efcwcuytgvc"></a>Miscarriage

If the pregnancy health reaches 0 at any point, they will experience miscarriage for 40 seconds instead, where attack speed is reduced by 50% and movement speed is reduced by 100%\. Afterwards, the entities get their babies as dead fetuses, and enter the same process as after giving birth, except for Luck effect changed to Unluck effect\.

### <a id="_7psyke3tvgiv"></a>Belly Burst

If the pregnant entity reaches the end of phase 8 while carrying more than 8 babies, or when too many babies are being carried by one womb, a belly burst will trigger instead, where a small explosion triggers on spot, and the entity’s belly pops\. This instantly kills the entity \(even if holding a Totem of Undying, which wastes it\)\.

Due to natural pregnancies cannot result in a pregnancy with more than 8 babies, this is only achievable with misuse of Baby Duplication effect\.
