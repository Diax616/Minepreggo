# <a id="_h3jl2go1rwwk"></a>__Advancements Guide__

This guide provides an overview of all the advancements available in the Minepreggo mod for Minecraft Forge 1\.20\.1\.

## <a id="_rfva7z315nau"></a>Starter Advancements

The advancements do not show up initially; Instead, the player needs to first acquire the first advancement of the advancement tree\. Each gender has a separate tree\.

### <a id="_rzosthrwphgn"></a>Female Branch: I’m Pregnant?

To obtain this advancement, the player must be a female, and get pregnant through either player sex, villager sex, attacks from zombies, creepers, or endermen, or through potions or the Reproduction Witch\. The type and number of babies do not matter\.

This is the starting point for most pregnancy\-related advancements\.

### <a id="_139q9ddtua9i"></a>Male Branch: Take Responsibility

To obtain this advancement, the player must be a male, and impregnate any female entities, including but not limited to female players, zombie girls, creeper girls \(humanoid or monster\) and ender girls\. The type and number of babies do not matter\.

This marks the beginning of the father\-related advancement tree\.

### <a id="_edp334n8r3ym"></a>Shared Branch: Interested in the End

To obtain this advancement, the player must obtain a Concentrated Ender Cum Specimen Tube\. The gender of the player does not matter\.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the Adventure section of vanilla Minecraft advancements\.

## <a id="_bd10y2uaromm"></a>Female Branch Advancements

Once the player gets pregnant, they will gain access to the entire advancement tree\. The sequence does not matter, so one can acquire them in any order\.

### <a id="_4ikic41ydvlu"></a>First Time Mother

To obtain this advancement, the player must successfully be impregnated through any means and successfully give birth without death or miscarriage during pregnancy, with either losing the babies in the process\. The type and number of babies do not matter\.

### <a id="_7t8zd4ueb9w9"></a>Magic Pregnancy

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to be impregnated with potion effects, whether it is from a potion or an arrow\. The type or number of babies does not matter\.

### <a id="_8ymchqvnejeg"></a>Good Mother

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to complete all three types of prenatal checkups on themselves, including regular checkup, ultrasound scan and paternity test\. This can be done from a Doctor or a Scientific Illager\.

As players __cannot__ do checkups without being pregnant, they have to be impregnated before attempting\.

### <a id="_m2usuw4uiutr"></a>Bad Mother

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to sell a baby item born by self to a Scientific Illager\. The type of babies does not matter\.

### <a id="_ng0jjdhqpenl"></a>Breeder of the Village

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to be impregnated by a villager naturally\. The type or number of babies afterwards do not matter\.

Cheating by using Villager Impregnation potions __won’t__ trigger the advancement\.

### <a id="_1xvm1p8jxpxs"></a>New Member for the Village

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to use a baby villager on the villager that impregnated the player\.

As pregnancy through potions does not register a villager, babies from such are not usable for this advancement\.

### <a id="_vldbx7j1csx"></a>Brewing an Ender Dragon Potion

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to brew a potion of Ender Dragon Impregnation\.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the End section of vanilla Minecraft advancements\.

### <a id="_svdmkbnr2qyv"></a>Pregnant with a Dragon

To complete this challenge, the player needs to be impregnated with an Ender Dragon baby\. The way of such does not matter\.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the End section of vanilla Minecraft advancements\.

### <a id="_1412xcc3ufkc"></a>Give Birth a Dragon

To complete this challenge, the player needs to successfully endure and give birth to the Ender Dragon baby\. Any potion usage in the period is allowed\. Due to the rarity of the potion itself, the player was not permitted death in this period, or the challenge will be considered incompletable without uses of cheats\.

Unlike other advancements of this mod, this can be found in the End section of vanilla Minecraft advancements\.

### <a id="_nk1itbc51di"></a>Fertile Mother

To complete this challenge, the player needs to be impregnated with either player or villager sex, or from attacks of zombies, creepers or endermen, and has 8 babies as a result\. The type of babies does not matter\.

Cheating by using Impregnation potions or Baby Duplication potions won’t trigger the advancement\.

### <a id="_oewmmhi50ai0"></a>Give Birth by All Species

To complete this challenge, the player needs to give birth to human, villager, zombie, creeper, enderman, and the Ender Dragon at least once each\. The way to be impregnated does not matter\.

Due to the difficulty of interspecies pregnancy and the rarity of ender dragon pregnancy, it is recommended to challenge this as the final objective of the mod\.

## <a id="_76m9yqqsq9xv"></a>Male Branch Advancements

Once the player impregnates an entity, they will gain access to the entire advancement tree\. The sequence does not matter, so one can acquire them in any order\.

### <a id="_bmdhjaaykunk"></a>Bad Father

To obtain this advancement, the player needs to sell a baby item born by the entity that’s impregnated by themselves to a Scientific Illager\. The type of babies does not matter\.

### <a id="_yil9vy1i9dtp"></a>Impregnate All Species

To complete this challenge, the player needs to impregnate female players, zombie girls, creeper girls \(both humanoid and monster\) and ender girls\. The outcome of pregnancy does not matter\.

Unlike most of the advancements of this mod, this is __incompletable__ under single player\.
