# <a id="_v4dx1lrze53g"></a>Milk and Potion Effect Guide

This guide explains how milk works in the Minepreggo mod and the special properties of breast milk items\.

## <a id="_m6ert7dvqf77"></a>Modifications

As known to players, milk buckets from Minecraft can be used to clear any effects the user has, whether it’s positive or negative, and includes most effects from other mods\. However, these are not true in this mod; Some effects are __not__ cleanseable with milk\.

## <a id="_cyxo82umvspj"></a>Breast Milk Bottles

These are naturally obtainable by using a glass bottle on any pregnant entity at phase 2 onwards, with the type depending on the entity\.

Unlike regular milk, these can recover 3 hunger and 1 saturation on consumption along with cleansing most effects\. The only exception is ender breast milk bottles, which recover 5 hunger instead\.

The user also gains side effects on consumption:

- Human Breast Milk Bottle \- A brief Regeneration
- Zombie Breast Milk Bottle \- A brief Hunger and Poison II
- Creeper Breast Milk Bottle \- An explosion triggers on spot
- Ender Breast Milk Bottle \- Teleports to a spot randomly, like a chorus fruit

They’re also the main ingredients for most pregnancy\-related potions\.
