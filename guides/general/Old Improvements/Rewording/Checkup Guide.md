# <a id="_wqpqozjeblw3"></a>__Medical Checkups Guide__

This guide explains how medical checkups work in the Minepreggo mod, including how to activate them, who provides them, and what benefits they offer\.

## <a id="_qc343e8t3u86"></a>Overview

Medical checkups are special services offered by Doctors or Scientific Illagers to help monitor and assess pregnancy\. During pregnancy, players and tamed pregnant mobs can visit these NPCs to receive valuable information and items related to their pregnancy status\.

## <a id="_t0zp7fwl2746"></a>Types of Medical Checkups

There are two different types of medical checkups available, with up to 5 services in total\. All of them cost emeralds and can be done by either a Doctor or a Scientific Illager\. However, the Doctor __cannot do__ any of them on preggo mobs\.

### <a id="_j0kzz9mq4rlu"></a>Fertility Test

This type of checkup is only accessible when the patient is not pregnant\. There are two services:

- Fertility State \- This informs the user about their fertility level, their current menstrual phase and the time required for specific periods\.
- Pregnancy Test \- This detects whether the user is ongoing in the fertilisation process\.

### <a id="_7xm88xdabybq"></a>Prenatal Checkup

This type of checkup is only accessible when the patient is pregnant\. There are three services:

- Pregnancy State \- This informs the user about the current pregnancy phase, pregnancy health, and days left before the next phase or giving birth\. At least pregnancy phase 1 is required\.
- Ultrasound Scan \- This detects the baby count, belly safety and the baby species and gender\. At least pregnancy phase 2 is required\.
- Paternity Test \- This detects the entity that impregnated the user\. If there is no entity \(which happens with potion pregnancy\), none will be stated\. At least pregnancy phase 2 is required\.
