# <a id="_x9vtyr1ph3o1"></a>__Pregnancy Potion Guide__

This guide explains the various pregnancy\-related potions that can be thrown by the Reproduction Which and used throughout the Minepreggo mod\.

## <a id="_rpvej1x9o9aa"></a>Overview

The Minepreggo mod contains several special potions that affect pregnancy in different ways\. These potions can be obtained from the Reproduction Witch, brewed through the alchemy system, or found as loot\. Each potion serves a unique purpose in managing, assisting, or harming pregnancy\.

## <a id="_xtluq6o32f1z"></a>Base Potions

These potions act as the base potion for most pregnancy potions, however they have their own effects too\.

### <a id="_czuhbea9fze2"></a>Potion of Fertility

This potion increases the fertility score and recovers reproductive health for the user\. In version 1\.3\.0, this also shift the fertility cycle for female entities into ovulation, as well as increasing number of eggs\. It also increase chance for splits for female entities, and increase chance for fertilizing for male entities\.

To brew a potion of Fertility, add a potion of Instant Health II with Human Breast Milk Bottle\. In version 1\.3\.0, however, this recipe is removed\.

Potions of Fertility can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches, as loot from chests, or traded from a Doctor\.

### <a id="_nqgiknriyclt"></a>Potion of Infertility

This potion reduces the fertility score and reproductive health for the user\. In version 1\.3\.0, it also removes all eggs, making impregnation impossible\.

To brew a potion of Infertility, add a potion of Instant Damage II with Zombie Breast Milk Bottle\. In version 1\.3\.0, however, this recipe is removed\.

Potions of Infertility can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches\.

## <a id="_pyzlp0mh5azb"></a>Pregnancy Initialising Potions

These potions on consumption make the target pregnant, with the type of babies fully dependent on the potion type, with the number of babies depending at the level of the potion:

| Level | Babies |
|-------|--------|
| 1     | 1      |
| 2     | 2-3    |
| 3     | 4-5    |
| 4     | 6-7    |
| 5     | 8-9    |
| 6     | 10     |

The user needs to wait for the potion effect to expire for pregnancy, however due to how effects work in the game, simply drinking the same potion again will instantly trigger pregnancy\.

All potions are __ineffective__ to every other mobs except female players and preggo mobs\.

### <a id="_8ew1t9z3ltov"></a>Potion of Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with babies of the same species\.

To brew a potion of Impregnation, simply add a Regeneration potion with a Human Cum Specimen Tube\.

Potions of Impregnation can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches or traded from a Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_g0p3m1961rsb"></a>Potion of Zombie Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with purely zombie babies\. This cannot be used on creeper girls and ender girls\.

To brew a potion of Zombie Impregnation, add a potion of Impregnation with a Zombie Cum Specimen Tube\.

Potions of Zombie Impregnation can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches or traded from a Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_bdvj8qns9u7j"></a>Potion of Creeper Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with purely creeper babies\. This cannot be used on zombie girls and ender girls\.

To brew a potion of Creeper Impregnation, add a potion of Impregnation with a Creeper Cum Specimen Tube\.

Potions of Creeper Impregnation can also be traded from a Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_ajjx1p9m7e54"></a>Potion of Humanoid Creeper Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with purely humanoid creeper babies\. This cannot be used on zombie girls and ender girls\.

In 1\.2\.10, this potion has a 50% chance to replace potions of Creeper Impregnation when brewing\.

This potion is no longer __obtainable__ in 1\.3\.0\.

### <a id="_uajabftwz1wg"></a>Potion of Ender Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with ender babies\. This cannot be used on zombie girls and creeper girls\.

To brew a potion of Ender Impregnation, add a potion of Impregnation with a Ender Cum Specimen Tube\.

### <a id="_2qtly73vf9qq"></a>Potion of Humanoid Ender Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with humanoid ender babies\. This cannot be used on zombie girls and creeper girls\.

In 1\.2\.10, this potion has a 50% chance to replace potions of Ender Impregnation when brewing\.

This potion is no longer __obtainable__ in 1\.3\.0\.

### <a id="_wiq7rypsnk6h"></a>Potion of Ender Powerful Impregnation

This is an enhanced version of potions of Ender Impregnation, where the user will guarantee to be pregnant with 8 ender babies, and receive a brief period of Regeneration on top of a long period of Resistance\. This cannot be used on zombie girls and creeper girls\.

To brew a potion of Ender Powerful Impregnation, add a potion of Impregnation with Concentrated Ender Cum Specimen Tube\.

It is not recommended to drink this potion due to its cost; Its main purpose is as the base potion for another brewing session\.

### <a id="_g2s80ivwrr1m"></a>Potion of Ender Dragon Impregnation

This potion allows the user to be pregnant with Ender Dragon baby\. Only female player can use it\.

To brew a potion of Ender Dragon Impregnation, add a potion of Ender Powerful Impregnation with the dragon egg\.

As the dragon egg can__ only be obtained once__ in the game, this is an extremely rare potion to have, hence use it wisely\.

## <a id="_jnw9udyljxyl"></a>Pregnancy Managing Potions

These potions help to change the current state of pregnancy, including the growth and count of babies\.

Do note that using these potions may change the pregnancy phase based on situations\.

### <a id="_8r82m3gnv7ho"></a>Potion of Pregnancy Delay

This potion delays the birth date of babies, making pregnancy longer\.

The delayed time is listed as follows:

| Pregnancy Delay Level | From Total Days |
|-----------------------|-----------------|
|           1           |        8%       |
|           2           |       16%       |
|           3           |       24%       |
|           4           |       32%       |
|           5           |       40%       |

To brew a potion of Pregnancy Delay, add a potion of Infertility with Rotten Flesh\.

Potions of Pregnancy Delay can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches\.

### <a id="_9xvxzc03x6c"></a>Potion of Pregnancy Acceleration

This potion shortens the birth date of babies, making pregnancy shorter\.

The reduced time is listed as follows:

| Pregnancy Acceleration Level | From Total Days |
|-----------------------|-----------------|
|           1           |        8%       |
|           2           |       16%       |
|           3           |       24%       |
|           4           |       32%       |
|           5           |       40%       |

To brew a potion of Pregnancy Acceleration, add a potion of Fertility with Glistering Melon Slice\.

Potions of Pregnancy Acceleration can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches or traded from a Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_8edoppbhjmj0"></a>Potion of Baby Duplication

This potion increases the baby count in the current pregnancy, without changing the type of babies\.

Each level of the potion duplicates 1 baby\. The womb has a capacity, however, so misusing this potion may lead to __deadly consequences__ \(check Pregnancy Mechanic Guide\)\. Being pregnant with Ender Dragon baby nullifies the effect entirely\.

Unlike most potions, this __cannot__ be brewed, but as a rare drop from Reproduction Witches\.

### <a id="_x8p04voinxq4"></a>Potion of Eternal Pregnancy

This potion completely pauses the pregnancy phase and completely disables the effects of Pregnancy Delay and Pregnancy Acceleration\. This also increase the chance per tick of fetal movement to 5 times of original chance\. This lasts until the death of the user, miscarriage, or drinking milk\.

Unlike most potions, this __cannot__ be brewed, but as drops from Reproduction Witches\.

## <a id="_h7viv7ooa0ru"></a>Other Pregnancy\-Related Potions

These potions do not directly affect the progress of pregnancy but serve as protection and assistance to make the pregnancy safe and comfortable\.

### <a id="_kg1czogwz1rl"></a>Potion of Pregnancy Resistance

This potion grants the user protection for their babies by nullifying pregnancy damage\.

To brew a potion of Pregnancy Resistance, add a potion of Strength with Human Breast Milk Bottle\.

Potions of Pregnancy Resistance can also be traded from a Doctor or Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_tt3g3z4cm7e4"></a>Potion of Pregnancy Healing

This potion recovers the pregnancy health of the user\.

To brew a potion of Pregnancy Healing, add a potion of Instant Health with Human Breast Milk Bottle\.

Potions of Pregnancy Healing can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches or traded from a Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_8k0wpqu0qc71"></a>Potion of Pregnancy Harming

This potion reduces the pregnancy health of the user\. This is only advised to be consumed if the player wants to conduct miscarriage\.

To brew a potion of Pregnancy Harming, add a potion of Instant Damage with Zombie Breast Milk Bottle\.

Potions of Pregnancy Harming can also be found as drops from Reproduction Witches\.

### <a id="_wpu0v79j567s"></a>Potion of Metabolism Control

This potion reduces the increase in hunger from pregnancy\.

To brew a potion of Metabolism Control, add an Awkward Potion with a Fungus\. This can be Crimson Fungus or Warped Fungus\.

Potions of Metabolism Control can also be traded from a Doctor or Scientific Illager\.

### <a id="_sbg522yawtbe"></a>Potion of Zero Gravity Belly

This potion nullifies the gravity effect from the big belly, including the falling and knockback\.

To brew a potion of Zero Gravity Belly, add a potion of Slow Falling with Human Breast Milk Bottle\.

Potions of Zero Gravity Belly can also be traded from a Doctor or Scientific Illager\.

## <a id="_5xou7mqt8s2a"></a>Enhancements

As with vanilla potions, pregnancy potions can be enhanced on their effects and durations\.

For pregnancy initialising potions and pregnancy managing potions, one can upgrade their effect with following:

- Level 2 \- Glowstone Dust
- Level 3 \- Nether Quartz Ore
- Level 4 \- Blaze Rod
- Level 5 \- Ender Pearl
- Level 6 \- Ghast Teat

The only exception is the potion of Ender Powerful Impregnation, potion of Ender Dragon Impregnation, and potion of Eternal Pregnancy, which cannot be upgraded at all\.

For other pregnancy potions, adding Redstone can extend the duration, except for potion of Pregnancy Healing and potion of Pregnancy Harming, which has no duration\.

While the potion of Metabolism Control II is present in the Creative Mode menu, it is __unobtainable__ in Survival Mode\.
