# <a id="_3n6ea2dfibc0"></a>__New Mobs Guide__

This guide explains the new humanoid mobs, changes of existing humanoid mobs, along with their properties and drops\.

## <a id="_ksrjd39lgbp"></a>Villagers

All adult villagers now gain the reproduction system similar to male players; They can have sex with female players, and have their own fertility rate and sperm concentration, both of which affect the amount of villager babies the player have to carry\. They also now have villager brains as drops\.

### <a id="_gykrdv2dauso"></a>Trades

Trades available for most villagers are the same as before, with the only exception of farmers and butchers\.

For farmers, the player can buy chilli pepper, lemon, cucumber, cocoa beans and chocolate bar from them, while the player can sell chilli pepper, lemon and cucumber to the farmers\. The selling price is lower than the purchase price, hence the player cannot abuse it for infinite emeralds\.

For butchers, the player can buy most human foods, excluding lemon drops, french fries and chilli poppers, as well as hot sauce\. The player cannot sell anything to them, however\.

### <a id="_mvybgs1mimr6"></a>Doctor

The Doctor is a new profession for the villagers\. This is available after using a medical table as the workbench\.

From a Doctor, the player can buy most of the pregnancy assisting potions \(except for potions of Metabolism Control\), as well as potions of Fertility, Instant Health and Instant Health 2\. They're also the main source of human cum specimen tube \(if single player\) and villager cum specimen tube\. The player can only sell human cum specimen tubes, with a lower price than the purchase price, hence female players cannot abuse it\.

They also possess the ability to conduct medical checkups, however they can only do so to female players\. They also can impregnate female players like other villagers when conditions are met\.

## <a id="_wtboik9c935o"></a>Illagers

Illagers, or raiders, do not have any changes compared to the original, including their activities\. However, they now drop illager brains as an additional drop\.

### <a id="_keqv3vijgdev"></a>Scientific Illager

This unique variant of Illager, having much higher intelligence than other raiders, is only found in the naturally generated Scientific Illager Hideout structure, usually in forests\.

Unlike other raiders, the player can sell babies for diamonds, fetuses for emeralds or cum specimen tubes of specific species, or milk bottles for diamonds or gold ingots\. They can also buy most impregnation and pregnancy assisting potions \(except for potions of Ender Impregnation\), as well as potions of Pregnancy Acceleration\. The player can also exchange enchanted books\.

Some notable trades are with a dead humanoid creeper or humanoid ender fetus, which can give either a netherite ingot, a stack of emeralds, or cum specimen tubes of the corresponding species\. They can also sell the baby ender dragon block for a special gift that indicates the completion of the mod\.

Scientific Illagers usually spawn along with ill preggo mobs of each species, which are identical to the healthy variants, except they lack the ability to be impregnated, and only attack when provoked or when the Illager is dead\. The Illager also attack like a vindicator or a normal Illager when provoked and have the possibility to spawn with the ominous banner \(which gives the player bad omen if they are killed\)\.

## <a id="_q8xzx7x2rn5a"></a>Witches

Witches do not have any notable changes; They still attack with potions and spawn at the same condition\. However, they now drop witch brains as extra drops\.

### <a id="_r6nv4hlj6hhf"></a>Reproduction Witches

Reproduction Witches \(named Fertility Witches before 1\.3\.0\) are a unique variant of witches, where they possess the ability to throw pregnancy related potions\. There are two variants; The Fertility Witch \(pink variant\) and the Infertility Witch \(grey variant\)\. They can spawn in plains like witches, however they only spawn during full moon, and does not appear in raids, unlike normal witches\.

Fertility Witches possesses the ability to throw impregnation potions, as well as potions of Pregnancy Acceleration or Baby Duplication, on female entities, including preggo mobs\. If the target is a male player, however, they behave identical to witches\. They are more likely to spawn in sunflower plains\.

For Infertility Witches, they possess the ability to throw potions of Infertility to prevent entities from pregnancy\. This works on male entities\. They are more likely to spawn in swamps\.

Their drops include the potions of Impregnation and Zombie Impregnation, potions of Pregnancy Harming, and potions of Fertility and Infertility\. Fertility Witches have a chance to drop potion of Baby Duplication, while the Infertility Witches have a chance to drop potion of Eternal Pregnancy, however both of those can only be obtained when the final blow is done by a player\.
