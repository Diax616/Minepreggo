# <a id="_hkt2who6835p"></a>__Other Species Pregnancy Guide__

This guide explains pregnancies for babies of other species in the Minepreggo mod, including the unique effects that occur when a player carries offspring from different creature species\.

## <a id="_rjvhh8f636dk"></a>Overview

Unlike preggo mobs, female players __can be__ pregnant with other species, which includes hostile mobs such as zombies, creepers or endermen, or special mobs such as villagers and the Ender Dragon\. All of them have a different effect when carrying their babies\.

## <a id="_jo1li4ovcdlp"></a>Interspecies Pregnancy

These are generally __harmful __to pregnant players and mostly come from hostile mobs\. Exercise caution to prevent these from happening unless one really wishes to be pregnant with those\.

### <a id="_a8j142t8xssl"></a>Zombie

Players are impregnated with zombie babies after attacks from zombies, husks, drowneds or zombie villagers, or from Zombie Impregnation effect\.

When carrying zombie babies until phase 3 onwards, fetal movement chance per tick is 140% of the original chance, while exhaustion from actions is 5% higher than when carrying human babies\. Random weakness also triggers 30 seconds faster than normal pregnancy\.

Not only that, being pregnant with zombie babies brings the Full of Zombies effect from phase 3, where the player will be afraid of sunlight, similar to a zombie, and starts burning when exposed to it\. This can be countered by using a helmet, just like how zombies do\. They may also have zombie foods as craving, with a 20% penalty on gratification\.

### <a id="_csnoagrltauc"></a>Creeper

Players are impregnated with creeper babies after attacks from creepers, or from Creeper Impregnation or Humanoid Creeper Impregnation effect\.

When carrying creeper babies until phase 3 onwards, fetal movement chance per tick is 180% of the original chance, while exhaustion from actions is 15% higher than when carrying human babies\. Random weakness also triggers 20 seconds faster than normal pregnancy\.

Not only that, being pregnant with creeper babies brings the Full of Creepers effect from phase 3, where when the player dies, an explosion occurs on spot, similar to a creeper\. The explosion effect is further amplified based on the current pregnancy phase, which makes it as destructive, or even __more destructive __than a charged creeper\. They may also have creeper foods as craving, with a 30% penalty on gratification\.

### <a id="_7cw2mko8wmtu"></a>Ender

Players are impregnated with ender babies after attacks from endermen, or from Ender Impregnation or Humanoid Ender Impregnation effect\.

When carrying ender babies until phase 3 onwards, fetal movement chance per tick is 220% of the original chance, while exhaustion from actions is 25% higher than when carrying human babies\. Random weakness also triggers 10 seconds faster than normal pregnancy\.

Not only that, being pregnant with ender babies brings the Full of Ender effect from phase 3, where the player teleports randomly every 2 minutes, similar to an enderman\. What is different from an enderman, however, is that it teleports fully __randomly__, and can send a player into a pit or lava\. The rate of teleportation depends on the number of babies carried and the current pregnancy phase\. They may also have ender foods as craving, with a 10%\-20% penalty on gratification\.

## <a id="_kr0k73u3n6uu"></a>Villager Pregnancy

Female players cannot initiate sex to villagers with just a right click, and male villagers do not possess the ability to initiate sex or attack to impregnate female players\.

### <a id="_uhcow263fq2w"></a>Impregnation and Pregnancy

To initiate villager breeding, players have to throw any valid food towards a villager, and they will attempt to pick up\. When the villager has 3 breads, or 12 carrots, beetroots or potatoes, they will consume it and become willing to breed\.

If the player is male, and two villagers receive the food, they will breed with each other as with usual\. If the player is female, is less than 25 blocks away \(if the player runs away after dropping the food for some reasons\), and if a bed is in 8\-block range, the villager instead breeds with the player\. After a short cutscene, the female player receives their sex side effects \(Weakness and Hunger II\) while the villager receives Slowness effect instead\. Whichever the case of breeding, those villager\(s\) will not breed again for 5 minutes\.

Using potions of Villager Impregnation skips the steps above entirely\. Carrying villager babies does not have any difference with human babies; All symptoms and processes are the same as with normal pregnancy, from phase 0 to giving birth\.

### <a id="_peym651qu6he"></a>Discounts and Reproduction

Villagers have a gossip value depending on the actions of players\. These are then converted to reputation with a multiplier, which is what decides the discount\.

Gossip decays every 20 minutes \(with exception of major positive gossip, major\+ for short for this guide\), and villagers are allowed to share gossip when the gossip exceeds the penalty \(it won’t reduce from sharing\)\.


|     Type       |     Action       |     Amount    |     Decay    |     Penalty    |     Max    |     Multiplier    |
|----------------|------------------|---------------|--------------|----------------|------------|-------------------|
|     Trading    |     Trading      |     2         |     2        |     20         |     25     |     x 1           |
|     Major+     |     Curing       |     20        |     0        |     100        |     100    |     x 5           |
|     Minor+     |     Curing       |     25        |     1        |     5          |     200    |     x 1           |
|     Major-     |     Killing      |     25        |     10       |     10         |     200    |     x-5           |
|     Minor-     |     Attacking    |     25        |     20       |     20         |     100    |     x-1           |


Breeding by default does not increase gossip in any way\. However, if a female player interacts with a villager in a way it is related to breeding, they may gain major\+ gossip\.

Initialising sex with a villager gains 5 major\+ gossip, while being pregnant with the babies of said villager gain 20 major\+ gossip\. Successfully giving birth to those babies gains a grand 30 major\+ gossip\. While all those only triggers __once __per villager, it saves a big hassle for requiring several instances of curing for a big discount\.

As for the villager babies \(not human babies\) that the player gave birth to, the player can right click on a villager, ideally the villager that impregnated you, while holding the baby villager\. This spawns a baby villager in a similar manner with two villagers breeding\. This can repeat __infinitely__, meaning a female player can repopulate or create a whole village with her womb, in a similar way of male player breeding villagers with just throwing food\.

## <a id="_n1ed6tf3ypdr"></a>Ender Dragon Pregnancy

While it is said as a pregnancy that carries Ender Dragon babies, the Ender Dragon is way too large to conduct sex or breed in any form, hence one has to use a workaround\.

The only way to be pregnant with an Ender Dragon baby is via the potion of Ender Dragon Impregnation, an extremely rare potion from brewing\. For the recipe of the potion, check the Potion Guide\. After drinking, the player will be pregnant with the baby after the effect expires, as with other impregnation potions\. Only female players can be pregnant with Ender Dragon babies\. Baby Duplication effect also do not work\.

### <a id="_vfs3uadg5u7l"></a>Pregnancy

Immediately upon being pregnant, the player will immediately feel the strength of the ender dragon, as they feel their health increased by 30%, and ender particles start emitting around their body 8% of the time\.

In phase 1, the player will start feeling the belly and symptoms as with usual pregnancies, however the hard skin of the ender dragon now spreads to their mother, giving \+20% armor\. Ender particles now emit 19% of the time\.

In phase 2, the belly is swollen enough for the player to feel gravity, but the dragon’s strength now reveals itself, as players’ attacks deal \+10% knockback to targets\. Ender particles now emit 29% of the time\.

Phase 3 is where the dragon carrying journey really starts; Due to the dragon is still not a type of human species, it also causes a lot of toll to the mother like other mobs, but more intense; Fetal movement chance per tick is 260% of the original chance, and exhaustion from actions increased by 40%\. This comes with its benefits, though, as random weakness triggers 20 seconds slower, and the hard skin further amplifies defense, giving Resistance effect and permanent Pregnancy Resistance effect, rendering miscarriage impossible without death\. The eye of the Ender Dragon was also inherited, giving the mother permanent Night Vision\. Ender particles now emit 39% of the time\.

Phase 4 further intensifies the dragon’s resistance to most non\-physical effects, giving Fire Resistance, Poison Immunity and Wither Immunity, making the mother invincible to all but normal attacks\. However, the dragon baby is bigger than other species, making the belly heavier, which makes the mother take \-20% knockback, but also applies a \-10% movement speed modifier and a \-10% attack speed modifier\. Ender particles now emit 48% of the time\.

Phase 5 further increases the belly’s weight, making it not only push off the maternity chestplates, but the mother also feels \+15% more gravity than usual pregnancies\. However, the dragon’s strength also gets amplified, giving the mother Strength effect\.

Ender particles now emit 55% of the time\.

Phase 6 is where the mother touched the real power of the dragon, namely the __ender essence__\. Every 65 seconds, the mother gains 1 ender power, and they can now use 2 ender power to teleport whether they want\. The hard skin now gives Resistance 2 effect, while the heavy belly now makes the mother take \-30% knockback\. Ender particles now emit 61% of the time\.

Phase 7 showed the dedication of the mother to reach this far, and the endermen now senses the existence of the dragon, hence recognising the mother as their protection target\. The mother no longer angers the endermen, even when staring at them, and instead they will fight for the mother once threat appears\. The dragon’s regenerative ability also applied to the mother, who gained the Regeneration effect\. Ender power regenerates every 45 seconds, and ender particles now emit 67% of the time\.

The last phase of the pregnancy leaves the mother with an extremely huge belly, making them take \-40% knockback on top of all the weight applied with this belly\. This, however, also fully reveals the dragon’s strongest ability, where the mother can touch the essential __ender dragon essence\. __They can now use 4 ender power to shoot fireballs, like a ghast\. The mother also gains Strength 2 effect and Regeneration 2 effect as the dragon is close to being born\. Ender power now regenerates every 30 seconds, and ender particles now emit 71% of the time\.

After that, the water breaks and the mother is going to be giving birth to the dragon baby\!

### <a id="_fy2cfcfgyrnz"></a>After Birth

The mother receives a baby ender dragon which, unlike other babies, can be put onto the world as a block, or sell it to the Scientific Illager for a special gift\.
