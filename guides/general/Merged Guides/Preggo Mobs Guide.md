# Preggo Mobs Guide

This guide explains the special hostile/tamable mobs, named PreggoMobs, and their behaviors in the Minepreggo mod in v1.3.1.

## Overview

PreggoMobs at first glance are not much different to normal hostile mobs; They attack and react to the environment and threats the same way as other mobs, with the real exception being they're female. However, they are tamable in one way or another, allowing them to be companions instead of enemies, regardless of the player's gender. Also, because they are female entities, they have the pregnancy system identical to female players', whether they are tamed or hostile.

To tame a PreggoMob, the player needs to find the food of respective species. Afterwards, they need to feed it to the preggo mob, like when taming animals. When successful, the PreggoMob is tamed and now follows the player. PreggoMob also no longer be hostile after being impregnated by potions.

### Hunger and Behavior

Unlike other mobs, tamed PreggoMobs possess hunger in a similar manner to players. They lose hunger naturally and increase further when moving or swimming. However, they do not have extra hunger when pregnant.

When hunger is 5 or above, the PreggoMobs will be in **passive** state, where they can be interacted with, fed, and they will conduct actions based on the current movement mode. If hunger reaches 4 or below, the PreggoMobs will instead be in **angry** state, where they now attack any mob every 12 seconds. If they target the owner, they will stop attacking them after one attack. This is dispelled when hunger reaches 5 or above again.

If hunger reaches 0, instead of taking hunger damage, the PreggoMobs will be in **savage** state instead, where the leash on them will be off, and the owner can no longer interact with them. They also do not move or attack, unless an entity does it first. This is only dispelled when hunger reaches 12 or above.

### Pregnancy and Miscarriage

Pregnancy for PreggoMobs function completely the same as players; Get impregnated by sex from male player, or from potions. Afterwards, they will experience the same pregnancy process as female players, from first phase to post birth.

The only difference, however, is that they do not have the ability to carry babies of other species. And because of this, they do not have a fetal movement chance increase, and because they do not get exhausted from actions (except sex), they also receive no exhaustion increase.

When pregnancy symptoms such as cravings, milking, and belly rubs fail to be relieved, the PreggoMobs may enter the angry state as listed above, along with the side effects. For belly rubbing, the PreggoMob must be facing their owner, and the owner must be crouching and empty-handed. They may also initiate sex themselves every 3 minutes if horny symptoms are triggered.

Miscarriage triggers in a similar manner as well; When pregnancy health reaches 0, they experience the same miscarriage effect as players. The same goes for belly burst and death, however some fetuses may be born as baby mobs instead.

## Type of Preggo Mobs

### Zombie Girls

As the female version of zombies, zombie girls behave the same as male zombies; They are afraid of sunlight, start burning if exposed to it, and they can break doors and also infect villagers.

To tame a zombie girl, simply feed them with brains; This includes villager brains, illager brains and witch brains. These are also the main ingredients for zombie foods for their cravings.

As a humanoid preggo mob, they possess the ability to break blocks and eat food in the food slot automatically. They also have an entire inventory, similar to players, and armor slots for protection. Chestplates and leggings restriction function the same as pregnant players when they are pregnant.

Zombie girls during pregnancy are identical to pregnant players in terms of symptoms. However, their product of milking is zombie breast milk instead of human breast milk, which is less beneficial as a nutrition source.

### Humanoid Creeper Girls

As the female version of creepers, with limbs grown, humanoid creeper girls behave the same as male creepers; They are afraid of ocelots or cats, and can explode whether they are close to their target, or after being ignited by flint and steel.

They can also be powered with lightning, where they gain +20% movement speed, +25% health, +2 armor, +3 attack damage, and obviously a stronger explosion. This only lasts for 40 minutes, though, unlike male creepers that last permanently.

They spawn with three attack types; Don't Explode mode, which has a 40% chance of spawning, where they will never explode actively; Fight and Explode mode, which has a 50% chance of spawning, where they only explode at below half HP; Explode mode, which has a 10% chance of spawning, where they explode immediately like a normal creeper. This can be changed after taming.

To tame a humanoid creeper girl, simply feed them with activated gunpowder. These are also the main ingredients for creeper foods for their cravings. Unlike other preggo mobs, however, the tamed humanoid creeper girl has 2 more health than when untamed.

As a humanoid preggo mob, they move slower and have lower health than their monster variant, but they possess the ability to break blocks and eat food in the food slot automatically. They also have an entire inventory, similar to players, and armor slots for protection. Chestplates and leggings restriction function the same as pregnant players when they are pregnant.

Humanoid creeper girls during pregnancy are identical to pregnant players in terms of symptoms. However, their product of milking is creeper breast milk instead of human breast milk, which sends the user flying on consumption and hence not recommended as a nutrition source.

What makes them dangerous, however, is that their babies and belly will affect their destruction capabilities when they explode. Notably, the radius increases when they reach pregnancy phase 3, and the intensity also increases when reaching pregnancy phase 5, during which they swell bigger before explosion. Explosion radius and intensity increase further at pregnancy phase 8, where they swell even bigger.

### Monster Creeper Girls

As the female version of creepers, monster creeper girls behave the same as male creepers; They are afraid of ocelots or cats, and can explode whether they are close to their target, or after being ignited by flint and steel.

They can also be powered with lightning, where they gain +20% movement speed, +25% health, +2 armor, +3 attack damage, and obviously a stronger explosion. This only lasts for 40 minutes, though, unlike male creepers that last permanently.

They spawn with three attack types; Don't Explode mode, which has a 40% chance of spawning, where they will never explode actively; Fight and Explode mode, which has a 50% chance of spawning, where they only explode at below half HP; Explode mode, which has a 10% chance of spawning, where they explode immediately like a normal creeper. This can be changed after taming.

To tame a monster creeper girl, simply feed them with activated gunpowder. These are also the main ingredients for creeper foods for their cravings. Unlike other preggo mobs, however, the tamed monster creeper girl has 6 more health than when untamed.

As a monster preggo mob, they move faster and have higher health than their humanoid variant, but they lack the ability to break blocks and eat food in the food slot automatically. They also have only 4 inventory slots, and only a helmet slot for decoration.

Monster creeper girls during pregnancy are identical to pregnant players in terms of symptoms. However, their product of milking is creeper breast milk instead of human breast milk, which sends the user flying on consumption and hence not recommended as a nutrition source.

What makes them dangerous, however, is that their babies and belly will affect their destruction capabilities when they explode. Notably, the radius increases by 1 when they reach pregnancy phase 2, and both the radius and intensity increase by 2 when reaching pregnancy phase 4, during which they swell bigger before explosion. Explosion radius and intensity increase by 3 at pregnancy phase 6, where they swell even bigger, and increases by 4 instead at pregnancy phase 8, where they swell even more bigger.

### Monster Ender Girls

As the female version of endermen, monster ender girls behave the same as male endermen; They are afraid of water, taking damage and teleports away when touched, and can teleport randomly and carry blocks. They also get angered when players stare at them, unless they are tamed.

To tame a monster ender girl, simply feed them with ender slime jelly or refined chorus shards. These are also the main ingredients for ender foods for their cravings. Unlike other preggo mobs, however, the tamed monster ender girl has 6 more health than when untamed.

Tamed monster ender girls are different to other preggo mobs; The owner can ride on them or teleport with them. When riding, they walk at 50% slower speed, but it saves the owner from exhaustion and jumping, as they can step up 1 block height easily. To ride on them, the owner must not have a big pregnant belly, which happens at pregnancy phase 3 onwards, and the ender girl must not be damaged, not experiencing pregnancy symptoms, and be in passive state. For teleportation, the owner must be close by to teleporting, and the ender girl must be following you, not experiencing pregnancy symptoms, and be in passive state. If all conditions are met, the owner can easily teleport anywhere with the ender girl.

As a monster preggo mob, they lack the ability to break blocks and eat food in the food slot automatically. They also have only 10 inventory slots, and only a helmet slot for decoration.

Monster ender girls during pregnancy are identical to pregnant players in terms of symptoms, though the owner must be having their eye level on the belly level for belly rubbing. However, their product of milking is ender breast milk instead of human breast milk, which teleports the user, like a chorus fruit. These are also the main ingredient for ender dragon pregnancy.

### Humanoid Slime Girls

_This PreggoMob is added since v1.3.0._

As the female version of slimes, with limbs grown, humanoid slime girls behave the same as male slimes; They bounce around, and spawn small slimes on defeat. What is different, however, is that the size of slime girls does not affect the size of small slimes.

As a slime, they also possess different size like male slimes, from normal, to big, huge and massive. Each stage increases health by 30%, armor by 1, attack by 2, knockback resistance by 2, and attack knockback by 2. It also increases their jumping power, both vertical and horizontal, by 0.1 per stage. To increase their size, the player needs to do a right click with a water bucket while they are full in hunger. This consumes the water to increase size by 1.

To tame a humanoid slime girl, simply feed them with stuffed mushrooms. These are, however, not used as slime foods for their cravings, but rotten fleshes and spider eyes are used instead. Unlike other preggo mobs, however, the tamed humanoid slime girl has 6 more health than when untamed.

As a humanoid preggo mob, they move slower and have lower health than their monster variant, but they possess the ability to break blocks and eat food in the food slot automatically. They also have an entire inventory, similar to players, and armor slots for protection. Chestplates and leggings restriction function the same as pregnant players when they are pregnant.

Tamed humanoid slime girls are different to other preggo mobs; The owner can ride on them. When riding, they walk at 50% slower speed, but it saves the owner from exhaustion and jumping, as they can step up 1 block height easily. To ride on them, the owner must not have a big pregnant belly, which happens at phase 3 onwards pregnancy, and the slime girl must be of big size, not having health below half, not experiencing pregnancy symptoms, and be in passive state.

Humanoid slime girls during pregnancy are identical to pregnant players in terms of symptoms. However, their product of milking is slime breast milk instead of human breast milk, which is less beneficial as a nutrition source.

Unlike other PreggoMobs, humanoid slime girls do not spawn naturally and can only be bought from a Scientific Illager.
