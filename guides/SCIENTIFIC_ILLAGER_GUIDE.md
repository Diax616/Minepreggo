## Overview

The **Scientific Illager** is a mysterious merchant figure who operates from hidden hideout structures. This entity serves as both a trader and an obstetrician, offering rare items related to pregnancy, breeding, and special supplies.

### Key Characteristics

- **Species:** Illager (hostile race)
- **Behavior:** Non-aggressive merchant (doesn't attack players)
- **Trading:** Offers rare and valuable items
- **Special Service:** Provides prenatal checkups for pregnant entities
- **Companions:** Spawns with four preggomob companions

## Prenatal Checkup Services

### Accessing Checkups

The Scientific Illager also provides **Prenatal Checkup** services for a fee.

**How to Access:**
1. **Sneak (Shift) + Right-Click** on the Scientific Illager
2. A menu will open showing nearby pregnant entities
3. Select the pregnant entity you want to check on
4. The checkup menu appears with service options

### Available Checkup Services

The Scientific Illager offers three types of checkups:

| Checkup Type | Cost | Information Provided |
|--------------|------|----------------------|
| Basic Checkup | 3 Emeralds | Pregnancy phase, health status |
| Standard Checkup | 10 Emeralds | Detailed information, duration estimate |
| Advanced Checkup | 15+ Emeralds | Complete analysis with recommendations |

**Compatible Entities:**
- Pregnant players
- Tamed pregnant preggomobs (within 16 blocks)
- Any entity implementing the pregnancy system

### Why Use Checkups

Prenatal checkups provide:
- Peace of mind about pregnancy status
- Information about pregnancy phase and progress
- Health monitoring
- Confirmation of successful pregnancy
