# Fertility Witch Guide

This guide explains the Fertility Witch entity, including the potions it throws, how it attracts mobs, and the valuable loot it drops upon defeat.

## Overview

The Fertility Witch is a dangerous hostile mob that specializes in pregnancy-related potions and effects. It behaves similarly to regular witches but has adapted to throw specialized potions related to fertility and pregnancy. These witches actively seek out pregnant entities and impregnate non-pregnant ones.

---

## Appearance and Basic Behavior

The Fertility Witch is a variant of the regular Minecraft witch with special abilities:

- **Cannot join raids** - Unlike regular witches, Fertility Witches will not participate in raids against villages
- **No XP reward** - Defeating a Fertility Witch provides no experience points
- **Higher step height** - Can climb up steep terrain more easily
- **Ranged attacker** - Throws potions at targets from a distance

## Special Pregnancy-Related Potions

### Against Pregnant Players

When a Fertility Witch encounters a pregnant player at range (6+ blocks away):
- 50% chance to throw pregnancy-affecting potions
- Randomly chooses between:
  - **Pregnancy Delay Potions** (more common)
  - **Pregnancy Acceleration Potions**
  - **Baby Duplication Potions** (10% chance instead of pregnancy potions)

### Against Non-Pregnant Players

When a Fertility Witch encounters a non-pregnant player at range (6+ blocks away):
- 50% chance to throw impregnation potions
- Randomly throws one of these harmful impregnation variants:
  - **Zombie Impregnation Potions** (any level)
  - **Creeper Impregnation Potions** (any level, may be humanoid variant)
  - **Ender Impregnation Potions** (any level, may be humanoid variant, rarest)

### Against Pregnant Mobs

The Fertility Witch can detect and target pregnant mobs:
- Throws **Pregnancy Delay** or **Pregnancy Acceleration Potions** or **Baby Duplication Potions** depending on its mood
- Affects pregnant zombie and creeper mobs

### Against Non-Pregnant Mobs

The Fertility Witch will attempt to impregnate non-pregnant mobs:
- Throws **Impregnation Potions** (any level)
- Can affect tame zombie girls and humanoid creeper girls

## Finding Fertility Witches

### Spawn Locations

Fertility Witches spawn in the Overworld like regular witches:
- In swamps and witch huts
- At night or in dark areas
- In caves and underground

### Difficulty

The difficulty setting affects:
- Spawn rates of Fertility Witches
- Potency of potions they throw
- Whether they throw potions at all

## Strategy Tips for Combat

### Defensive Strategies

1. **Wear protective armor** - Reduces potion damage
2. **Bring milk** - Can cleanse negative potion effects
3. **Healing potions** - Have plenty on hand to counter damage potions
4. **Distance fighting** - Use a bow or crossbow from range to avoid close-range weakness potions

### Offensive Strategies

1. **Melee approach** - Close the distance quickly to minimize potion throws
2. **Block the view** - Use terrain to block the witch's line of sight
3. **Multiple attackers** - Witches are distracted when facing multiple targets
4. **Nighttime** - Witches are more active at night

## Notable Features

### Pregnancy Potion Selection

The Fertility Witch randomly chooses whether to focus on **Pregnancy Delays** or **Pregnancy Accelerations** at the start of combat. This choice persists throughout the encounter.

### Player Detection System

The witch can:
- Detect if a player is pregnant through their data
- Differentiate between pregnant and non-pregnant players
- Target accordingly with different potion types

### Mob Interaction

The witch recognizes and can target various pregnant mob types:
- Pregnant zombie girls
- Pregnant creeper girls
- Humanoid variants of pregnant mobs
