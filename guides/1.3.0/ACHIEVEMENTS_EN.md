## Notes on Renamed Advancements

Two old advancements were split into two tiers. The **II** version keeps the original advancement; only the **I** version (listed above) is new:

| Original advancement | Now | Status |
|----------------------|-----|--------|
| Bad Mother | **Bad Mother II** (sell one of your own babies to a Scientific Illager) | Unchanged — same as always |
| Bad Father | **Bad Father II** (sell one of your partner's babies to a Scientific Illager) | Unchanged — same as always |

> Also, the **Fertile Mother** advancement changed its condition: it used to require 8 babies at once; now you get it with **more than 3 babies** from a single sexual encounter. Not a new advancement, but its requirement was relaxed.
