# Preggomob Taming Guide

This guide explains how to tame and befriend preggomobs in the Minepreggo mod.

---

## Overview

Certain preggomobs can be tamed and converted into domesticated versions that can be bred and managed. Taming allows you to build a farm or companion system with these creatures.

### Tamable Preggomobs

The following preggomobs can be tamed:
- **Zombie Girls** - Wild zombie variants
- **Humanoid Creeper Girls** - Humanoid creeper variants

Other preggomobs may not be tamable and will remain hostile/neutral.

---

## How to Tame a Preggomob

### Requirements

To tame a preggomob in survival mode, you will need:
1. **Species-Specific Food Item** (see table below)
2. The preggomob to be present in the world
3. An interaction (right-click with the food)

### Species-Specific Taming Foods

| Species | Taming Food | How to Obtain |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Zombie Girl | **Villager Brain** | Crafted from zombie drops, or obtained through other means |
| Humanoid Creeper Girl | **Activated Gunpowder** | Crafted from gunpowder (dropped by creepers) |

### Taming Process

**Step 1: Locate a Wild Preggomob**
- Find a wild/untamed Zombie Girl or Humanoid Creeper Girl
- These are typically found spawning naturally in the world
- They will be hostile when approached

**Step 2: Gather the Correct Taming Food**
- For Zombie Girls: Craft **Villager Brain** items
- For Creeper Girls: Hunt creepers, craft gunpowder into **Activated Gunpowder**
- Ensure you have at least one of the correct item

**Step 3: Right-Click with the Taming Food**
- Approach the preggomob while holding the taming food
- Right-click on the preggomob with the correct food item in your hand
- If successful, the preggomob will be tamed and converted
- The food item may be consumed in the process

### What Happens After Taming

When a preggomob is successfully tamed:

1. **Entity Conversion**
   - The wild preggomob converts into a domesticated version
   - For example: MonsterZombieGirl â†’ TamableZombieGirl

2. **Ownership**
   - The preggomob becomes YOUR companion
   - It recognizes you as its owner
   - Other players cannot command it (depends on server settings)

3. **Property Transfer**
   - All properties transfer to the tamed version:
     - Health (current health status)
     - Name (if you named it)
     - Inventory (items it was carrying)
     - Current targets (if it was fighting something)
     - Equipment (armor, tools, weapons)

4. **Behavior Change**
   - The preggomob becomes **non-hostile**
   - It will no longer attack you on sight
   - It becomes docile and manageable

5. **New Capabilities**
   - The tamed preggomob gains breeding capabilities
   - It can now produce offspring
   - It has a fertility system
   - It can become pregnant

## Tamed Preggomob Features

### Breeding

Once tamed, preggomobs can be bred with other tamed preggomobs or with players:

- **Player-Mob Breeding:** A player can impregnate the tamed preggomob
- **Offspring:** Tamed preggomobs that breed produce babies. (NOT WORKING)

### Pregnancy System

Tamed preggomobs have a full pregnancy system:
- They experience pregnancy phases (P0-P8)
- They develop pregnancy symptoms (hunger, cravings, milking)
- They require care and attention during pregnancy
- They give birth to babies after the pregnancy duration ends

### Inventory Management

Tamed preggomobs can carry items:
- They have an inventory system
- Items transfer when taming occurs
- Equipment is properly synced
- Items persist through saving/loading

### Customization

You can customize your tamed preggomob:
- **Name:** Use a name tag to give it a custom name
- **Equipment:** Dress it with armor or tools
- **Ownership:** It remains bound to you as the tamer

## Taming Tips and Best Practices

### Location Selection

1. **Safe Area:** Tame preggomobs in a safe, enclosed area
   - Prevents them from running away during conversion
   - Avoids interference from other mobs
   - Reduces risk of accidental death

2. **Designated Taming Space:** Create a small pen or enclosure
   - Make it easy to corral the preggomob
   - Minimize escape routes
   - Keep other mobs out

3. **Lighting:** Use adequate lighting
   - Prevents other hostile mobs from spawning
   - Makes it easier to see and interact
   - Keeps the area safe

### Preparation

1. **Farm Taming Food:**
   - Zombie Girls: Craft Villager Brain items (requires ingredients)
   - Creeper Girls: Hunt creepers for gunpowder, then craft Activated Gunpowder
   - Gather multiple items in case of failure or for future taming

2. **Clear the Area:**
   - Remove other hostile mobs nearby
   - Eliminate distractions
   - Create a calm environment

3. **Health and Safety:**
   - Ensure you have enough health
   - Keep weapons accessible
   - Have healing items (potions, food)

### During Taming

1. **Approach Carefully:**
   - Don't sprint directly at the preggomob
   - Give it space to avoid triggering attacks
   - Assess the situation before approaching

2. **Have Taming Food Ready:**
   - Hold the correct taming food in your main hand
   - Make sure it's not in your offhand
   - Have it selected before interacting

3. **Right-Click Interaction:**
   - Right-click directly on the preggomob
   - A successful tame happens immediately
   - The entity will convert and become tamed

### After Taming

1. **Verify Success:**
   - Check that the preggomob is now tamed (visual change)
   - Confirm it's no longer hostile
   - Verify your ownership

2. **Transport Home:**
   - Lead the tamed preggomob to your base
   - Use items it likes to lure it (if applicable)
   - Ensure a safe path

3. **Set Up Housing:**
   - Create a proper enclosure
   - Provide space for movement
   - Add appropriate decorations/amenities
   - Consider breeding space if planning offspring

## Common Taming Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Taming for Breeding

**Goal:** Create a breeding pair to farm offspring

**Steps:**
1. Tame two preggomobs of the same species in separate enclosures
2. Place them in adjacent spaces or a shared pen
3. Use impregnation potions to start breeding
4. Manage pregnancies of multiple preggomobs
5. Harvest offspring for farming

### Scenario 2: Taming for Companionship

**Goal:** Have a single tamed preggomob as a companion

**Steps:**
1. Tame a single preggomob
2. Name it with a name tag
3. Keep it with you in your base
4. Optionally breed it with yourself
5. Enjoy the pregnancy experience together

### Scenario 3: Taming for Experience

**Goal:** Breed preggomobs to earn experience and drops

**Steps:**
1. Tame multiple preggomobs
2. Create a breeding farm
3. Manage multiple pregnancies simultaneously
4. Collect resources from babies and deaths
5. Scale up breeding operation

## Tamed Preggomob Variants

After taming, your preggomob can exist in several states:

### Non-Pregnant State
- **Appearance:** Base form (non-pregnant belly)
- **Behavior:** Idle, wandering
- **Capabilities:** Can be impregnated, can breed

### Pregnant State
- **Appearance:** Progressive belly growth through pregnancy phases
- **Behavior:** Experiences symptoms (hunger, cravings, etc.)
- **Capabilities:** Cannot breed while pregnant, will give birth

### Post-Pregnancy State
- **Appearance:** Lactating/nursing appearance
- **Behavior:** Post-pregnancy recovery
- **Duration:** Temporary state before returning to non-pregnant
- **Capabilities:** Produces milk, limited movement

### Baby State
- **Appearance:** Smaller size, juvenile proportions
- **Behavior:** Passive, playful
- **Duration:** Grows into adult over time
- **Note:** Some gameplay mechanics may treat babies differently
