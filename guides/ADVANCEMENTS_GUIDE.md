## Starter Advancements

### I Am Pregnant
**Icon:** Leather Maternity Chestplate (P1)  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 20 XP

**How to Obtain:** Become pregnant for the first time using any method (sex or potion). You'll receive the pregnancy_p0 effect when successful.

This is the starting point for most pregnancy-related advancements.

### Take Responsibility
**Icon:** Baby Human  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 20 XP

**How to Obtain:** As a male player, successfully impregnate any entity from the impregnable entities list (Zombie Girls, Creeper Girls, Ender Girls, or other players).

This marks the beginning of the father-related advancement tree.

### Interested in the End
**Icon:** Concentrated Ender Life Substance  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 30 XP  
**Prerequisite:** Adventure Root

**How to Obtain:** Add concentrated ender life substance to your inventory. This substance is key to brewing special End-related potions.

**Icon:** Belly Shield (P5)  
**Type:** Challenge  
**Reward:** 50 XP  
**Prerequisite:** I Am Pregnant

**Prerequisite:** I Am Pregnant

**How to Obtain:** üíë **REQUIRES SEX PREGNANCY** - Become pregnant with a villager baby through natural interaction with a villager. This will NOT trigger if you use an impregnation potion.

**Icon:** Baby Villager

## Dragon-Related Advancements

### Brewing an Ender Dragon Potion
**Icon:** Baby Ender Dragon Block  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 50 XP  
**Prerequisite:** Dragon Egg (Vanilla)

**How to Obtain:** Use a brewing stand to create the ender dragon impregnation potion. This requires specific ingredients including concentrated ender life substance.

### Pregnant with a Dragon
**Icon:** Baby Ender Dragon Block  
**Type:** Challenge  
**Reward:** 100 XP  
**Prerequisite:** Brewing an Ender Dragon Potion

**How to Obtain:** Drink the ender dragon impregnation potion to become pregnant with a dragon baby. The pregnancy type must be specifically DRAGON to trigger this advancement.

This is one of the most difficult pregnancies to achieve.

### Give Birth a Dragon
**Icon:** Baby Ender Dragon Block  
**Type:** Challenge  
**Reward:** 200 XP  
**Prerequisite:** Pregnant with a Dragon

**How to Obtain:** Complete your dragon pregnancy and successfully give birth to a dragon baby. This is the highest-rewarding advancement in the mod!

Completes the ultimate pregnancy challenge!

## Care & Responsibility Advancements

### Good Mother
**Icon:** Golden Maternity Chestplate (P1)  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 50 XP  
**Prerequisite:** I Am Pregnant

**How to Obtain:** While pregnant, visit a doctor (Scientific Illager) and complete all three types of prenatal checkups on yourself:
- Regular checkup
- Ultrasound scan  
- Paternity test

Demonstrates excellent prenatal care!

### Bad Mother
**Icon:** Dead Human Fetus  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 30 XP  
**Prerequisite:** First Time Mother

**How to Obtain:** As a female player, trade away any baby item that you are verified as the parent of. This includes baby humans, zombies, creepers, enders, villagers, or dragon babies.

This morally questionable act earns you this advancement.

### Bad Father
**Icon:** Dead Human Fetus  
**Type:** Goal  
**Reward:** 30 XP  
**Prerequisite:** Take Responsibility

**How to Obtain:** As a male player, trade away any baby item that you are verified as the parent of. This includes any species of baby you fathered.

Like Bad Mother, this represents abandoning your parental responsibilities.

### Impregnate All Species
**Icon:** Baby Humanoid Ender  
**Type:** Challenge  
**Reward:** 100 XP  
**Prerequisite:** Take Responsibility

**How to Obtain:** As a male player, successfully impregnate at least one entity from each of the four species: Zombie Girls, Creeper Girls, Human (other players), and Ender Girls. The advancement tracks which species you've impregnated across all interactions.

## Tips for Advancement Hunters

**Pregnancy Methods Matter!**
- Some advancements specifically require **sex-based pregnancy** (üî•): Fertile Mother, Breeder of the Village
- Others require **potion-based pregnancy** (‚öóÔ∏è): Magic Pregnancy
- Most advancements work with either method
- Plan your pregnancy method carefully if targeting specific advancements!

**General Strategy:**
- Start with "I Am Pregnant" and "Take Responsibility" as these unlock most other advancements
- The dragon-related advancements are the most challenging and rewarding (200 XP for Give Birth a Dragon!)
- Complete all three prenatal checkups during one of your pregnancies to unlock "Good Mother"
- Challenge advancements offer the most XP but require dedication across multiple pregnancies
- For "Give Birth By All Species," you'll need to experience 6 different pregnancies
- For "Impregnate All Species," male players need to find and interact with all 4 impregnable entity types
- Some advancements have moral implications (Bad Mother/Father) - consider your roleplay choices carefully!

**Advancement Triggers:**
- Most pregnancy advancements trigger the moment you become pregnant (when you receive the pregnancy_p0 effect)
- Birth advancements trigger when you reach the final phase and complete delivery
- Checkup advancements require actually performing the medical procedures with a doctor
- Trade advancements trigger immediately when trading away a baby you're verified as parent of
- **Father advancements have a delayed trigger** - they fire when pregnancy initializes (several minutes after conception), not at the moment of conception

## Understanding Advancement Triggers

Different advancements trigger at different moments in gameplay:

**Pregnancy Start Triggers:**
- **I Am Pregnant** - Triggers immediately when pregnancy begins
- **Magic Pregnancy** - Triggers when pregnancy starts via potion
- **Fertile Mother** - Triggers when pregnancy starts with 8 babies via sex
- **Breeder of the Village** - Triggers when villager pregnancy starts via sex
- **Pregnant with a Dragon** - Triggers when dragon pregnancy begins

**Delayed Pregnancy Triggers:**
- **Take Responsibility** - Triggers when pregnancy **initializes** (several minutes after impregnation, not immediately)
- **Impregnate All Species** - Triggers when pregnancy **initializes** for each species (cumulative tracking)

**Birth Completion Triggers:**
- **First Time Mother** - Triggers after your first successful birth
- **Give Birth a Dragon** - Triggers after successfully birthing a dragon
- **Give Birth By All Species** - Triggers when you've birthed all 6 species (tracked cumulatively)

**Action-Based Triggers:**
- **Take Responsibility** - Triggers when you impregnate an entity
- **Impregnate All Species** - Triggers when you've impregnated all 4 species (tracked cumulatively)
- **Good Mother** - Triggers after completing all 3 prenatal checkup types
- **Bad Mother/Father** - Triggers when trading away your biological child
- **New Member for the Village** - Triggers when checking parentage with a villager
- **Interested in the End** - Triggers when obtaining concentrated ender life substance
- **Brewing an Ender Dragon Potion** - Triggers when brewing completes
